Sri Lanka Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) — Working Excerpts for Internal Review (non-authoritative)

- [PDPA-1] Definitions: "personal data" includes names, NIC numbers, and emails; "sensitive data" includes political opinions, ethnic origin, and religious beliefs.
- [PDPA-2] Explicit consent is required to process sensitive data unless a research derogation applies with documented ethics approval and appropriate safeguards.
- [PDPA-3] Retention should not exceed 24 months for personal or sensitive data, unless further retention is necessary for research with anonymization or pseudonymization measures documented.
- [PDPA-4] Cross-border transfers require adequate protection or standard contractual clauses and must be recorded in a transfer register.
