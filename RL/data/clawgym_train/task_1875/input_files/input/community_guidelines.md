# Community Tone & Safety Guidelines

- Use a peer-supportive, non-clinical tone. Prefer everyday language.
- Avoid clinical terms in posts: disorder, diagnosis, treatment plan.
- Keep each slide concise (aim for under 60 words).
- Do not give medical advice. Encourage seeking professional help when needed.
- Respect confidentiality and avoid identifiable details.
- Crisis Disclaimer (exact text to include): If you're in immediate danger or thinking about harming yourself, call 911 (or your local emergency number). In the U.S., you can call or text 988 for 24/7 free support.
