# Air Quality and Wildfire Smoke Advisory (rev 2024-05)

## Scope
Applies when wildfire smoke or regional pollution drives AQI categories Unhealthy (151–200) or worse.

## Indicators and Triggers
- AQI >= 151 (PM2.5) for 2+ hours.
- Verified reports of wildfire smoke plumes affecting the metropolitan area.

## Protective Actions
- Recommend N95 mask distribution at libraries and community centers.
- Encourage indoor air quality improvements: run HVAC on recirculate and use portable HEPA units where available.
- Advise cancellation or relocation of outdoor events for sensitive groups when AQI >= 151.

## Communications
- Publish plain-language guidance on AQI and PM2.5 for residents.
- Coordinate with schools to share mask and indoor air guidance.
