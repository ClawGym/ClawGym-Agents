Title: Weeknight Chicken Tacos
Category: Dinner
Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 20 minutes
Servings: 4

Description:
These are really easy, super fast chicken tacos that actually come together in just 30 minutes, with simple toppings your family will love.

Ingredients:
- chicken breast (1 lb)
- taco seasoning (2 tbsp)
- olive oil (1 tbsp)
- tortillas (8 small)
- lettuce (shredded)
- tomato (diced)
- cheese (shredded)
- sour cream

Directions:
1. Slice chicken and toss with taco seasoning.
2. Sauté in olive oil until cooked through.
3. Warm tortillas; prep toppings.
4. Assemble tacos and serve.
