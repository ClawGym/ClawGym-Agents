# Internal briefing: motorsport sponsorship options

- Cost comparison: early electric series budgets show higher cost per activation than our ICE benchmarks.
- Battery supply chain: complex certification requirements increase lead times and risk.
- Sustainability narrative: we must emphasize lifecycle analysis, not just tailpipe metrics.
- Fan engagement: tradition and sound remain key engagement drivers for our audience.
- EV demo days: trial interest is notable but conversion to fuel sales is unclear.
- Electric racing exposure: concentrated in urban ePrix; our fuel brand aligns better with endurance/ICE.
- Logistics: mobile charging units add cost and potential emissions if grid backup is diesel.
- Safety and training: high-voltage protocols require additional staffing and certification.
