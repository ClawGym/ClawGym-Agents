# Textiles on Paper
Stitching, knots, and layered threads sit against hand-sized sheets of paper, letting fiber behave a little differently than it does on stretched cloth. I keep a sketchbook of fragments and tests, and occasionally write about progress in the [studio feed](/studio-exhibitions).

These pieces feel intimate—many are held in the hand before they are framed. I explore how thread weight, paper sizing, and pressure translate into surface textures that change with light.
