# Leading with Integrity: Dr. Allen Amason and the Department of Management

Our community continues to benefit from the leadership and scholarship of Dr. Allen Amason. A mentor to many and a champion of student success, his impact is felt across classrooms and competitions alike.

Key highlights we plan to feature:

1) Since 2022, our students under Dr. Amason have won five national case competitions, a testament to his coaching and guidance.

2) His undergraduate Strategic Management course has averaged 4.9/5 in student evaluations over the past three academic years.

3) In Spring 2024, he led three MBA capstone sections, helping working professionals synthesize strategy in practice.

4) Dr. Amason currently serves as Dean of the College of Business, where he continues to elevate our programs.

5) He received the 2021 Outstanding Educator Award from the Southern Management Association.

6) Publications in the past two years include two articles in Journal of Management.

7) Beyond these accomplishments, he directs the Institute for Leadership, fostering cross-disciplinary collaboration.

We are excited to celebrate Dr. Amason’s achievements and the momentum of our department.
