# Branding Brief — Acme Clean

Product:
- Line of eco-friendly household surface cleaners: Multi-Surface, Kitchen, and Bathroom variants.
- Plant-based surfactants, dermatologist-tested, safe when used as directed.
- Recyclable bottles and refill pouches to reduce plastic waste.

Objectives:
- Establish a clear, credible positioning that differentiates from generic “green” claims.
- Create consistent brand voice and visuals across Amazon and Shopify.
- Improve conversion and reduce confusion between product variants.

Target Audience:
- Urban and suburban millennials (ages 25–40), renters and first-time homeowners.
- Care about sustainability but expect real cleaning performance.
- Busy schedules; want simple instructions and fast results.

Competitors:
- Method, Seventh Generation, Grove Co.
- Many emphasize “green” but underspec benefits; we will be concrete (e.g., “no residue,” “cuts grease fast”).

Key Messages:
- Powerful clean without harsh chemicals.
- Transparent ingredients and lab-validated performance.
- Better for your home and the planet when used as directed.

Tone & Personality:
- Helpful, pragmatic, friendly — avoid scare tactics and over-claiming.
- Informative and concise; rely on bullets and short headlines.

Visual Preferences:
- Clean, airy design with white space.
- Fresh green and ocean blue accents.
- Real-life home settings with natural light.

Constraints:
- Avoid unverifiable claims (e.g., “non-toxic” in absolute terms).
- Regional compliance: adjust spelling/units for local markets (e.g., UK vs US).

Deliverables:
- Positioning statement and RTBs (reasons to believe).
- Audience personas.
- Messaging framework for product pages and ads.
- Visual identity guidance (colors, typography, imagery).
