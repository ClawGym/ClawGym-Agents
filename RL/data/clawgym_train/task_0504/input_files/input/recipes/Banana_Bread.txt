Title: Banana Bread
Category: Snack
Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 55 minutes
Servings: 10

Description:
This is a really simple, super moist banana bread that actually just uses ripe bananas and basic pantry staples. Basically perfect for a cozy afternoon.

Ingredients:
- ripe bananas (3, mashed)
- sugar (3/4 cup)
- butter (1/2 cup, melted)
- egg (1 large)
- flour (1 1/2 cups)
- baking soda (1 tsp)
- salt (1/2 tsp)

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. Whisk wet ingredients, then stir in dry ingredients until just combined.
3. Pour into a greased loaf pan.
4. Bake 50–55 minutes, cool, slice, and enjoy.
