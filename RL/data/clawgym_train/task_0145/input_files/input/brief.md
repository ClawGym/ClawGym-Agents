Solo Sales Enablement Consultant — Brief

Who I serve
- Seed to Series B B2B SaaS companies
- Team size: 10–200 employees
- Locations: United States and Canada
- Buyers/partners: Head of Sales, VP Sales, RevOps (including RevOps Lead/Manager), Sales Manager

What I deliver
- Sales onboarding and ramp programs (30/60/90 with clear competencies)
- Enablement playbooks (discovery, qualification, MEDDICC, objection handling)
- Process and CRM hygiene (pipeline stages, definitions, dashboards; Salesforce/HubSpot alignment)
- Content and tooling for SDRs/AEs (talk tracks, email frameworks, call guides)
- Manager coaching and reinforcement cadences
- Change management for rollouts (training, certification, reinforcement loops)

Observable pain signals (prioritize leads showing 1+ of these)
- Hiring 2+ SDRs/AEs (team growth without enablement in place)
- Rep ramp > 90 days; inconsistent onboarding; content not used
- CRM migration (to/from Salesforce/HubSpot); messy data; low CRM adoption
- Low win rates; missed forecasts; weak discovery
- Tool sprawl; process gaps between SDR → AE → CS
- New funding (Seed/Series A/B) with pressure to scale GTM
- Public posts/podcasts about sales process challenges or enablement gaps
- Job posts for RevOps/Enablement indicating current pain

Disqualifiers (exclude)
- B2C
- Hiring freeze
- Pre-PMF or 0–1 reps
- Outside US/Canada for this campaign
- No budget/“DIY only” culture

Outreach tone and structure
- Personalization is mandatory: reference a recent job post, post, review, podcast, or CRM migration
- Keep emails under 100 words; lead with their outcome, not my features
- Ask for a low-commitment next step (“quick 10-min chat” or “send over a quick example”)
- Build a 5-touch sequence across LinkedIn and email over <=21 days
- Track stages: Identified → Contacted → Replied → In Conversation → Proposal Sent → Closed Won/Lost → Not Now

Volume targets
- 3–5 qualified new leads/week for this pilot sequence
- 1–3 discovery calls/week
