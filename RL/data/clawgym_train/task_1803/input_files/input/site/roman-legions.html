I am building a simple guide to legion organization, ranks, and routines so I can write scenes that feel grounded. The phrase roman legions daily life captures what I am after: not just battles, but food, punishments, tents, and who mended sandals after a march.

Marching practice, javelin drills, and fort building all reveal values like discipline and pride. Sources mention pay, retirement land, and the way soldiers decorated shields to show identity. I compare dates and look for contradictions that would make characters argue, learn, or change.
