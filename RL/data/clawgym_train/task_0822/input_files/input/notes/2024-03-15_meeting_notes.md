# Team Meeting — Publishing Trends — 2024-03-15

Attendees: Mina (chair), Jorge, Priya, Leah, Tom

## Agenda
- Submission trends and seasonal effects
- Open Access (OA) uptake and APC considerations
- Preprint policy updates and downstream analytics

## Discussion
- March is showing a healthy uptick in submissions. Open access share continues to rise across several journals.
- OA dashboards: stakeholders want a monthly Open Access snapshot with breakdown by discipline.
- Preprint policies: more editorial boards allow preprints without affecting peer review; potential impact on submission funnel quality.
- Peer review turnaround has improved; median review days decreased for Q1.
- Some concerns about APC communication and regional disparities in open access adoption.
- Preprints are increasingly cited; need to quantify the lag between preprint posting and journal acceptance.

Decision: Publish a monthly Open Access uptake report starting in April.
Decision: Keep the current peer review SLA targets for H1.

Action: Priya (analytics) to prototype Open Access dashboard by Mar 29.
Action: Jorge to analyze preprint citation lag and share preliminary findings by Apr 5.
Action: Leah to draft a short survey on peer review timelines for editors by Apr 1.
