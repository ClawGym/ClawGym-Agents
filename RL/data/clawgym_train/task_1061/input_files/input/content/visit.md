# Visit First United Church

Visiting us soon? We have Sunday gatherings and parking available near the building. Greeters are posted at entrances. Dress is casual to semi-formal.

## What to Expect

Expect music, readings, and a message. Childcare is available during services. For accessibility accommodations, please notify the office.

## Directions

We are located in Springfield near downtown; signage is posted around the block.
