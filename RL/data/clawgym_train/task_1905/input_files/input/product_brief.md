Title: ClearLeaf — Plant-Derived Home Care

Company & Product Scope
- Brand name: ClearLeaf
- Category: Eco-friendly household cleaners (concentrates + refillable bottles)
- Hero SKU: ClearLeaf Multipurpose Cleaner (500 ml)
- Secondary SKUs (Q3 roadmap ⚠️): Glass Cleaner, Bathroom Foam, Floor Concentrate
- Differentiators: Plant-based surfactants, biodegradable within 28 days, fragrance from natural essential oils, refill program with aluminum or glass bottles

Target Audience
- Primary: Environmentally conscious millennials and young families in urban/suburban areas
- Secondary: Pet owners sensitive to harsh chemicals; renters and condo dwellers seeking low-odor cleaners
- Tone & Voice: Calm, confident, evidence-led; avoid preachiness; practical sustainability

Channel Plan
- Amazon and Shopify at launch (D2C + marketplace)
- Retail partners (specialty eco stores) in evaluation ⚠️

Provisional Numbers (for planning; validate in first 30 days)
- MSRP (hero SKU): $12.99
- COGS (hero SKU): $3.80 (bottle $1.10, concentrate $1.90, label/pack $0.80)
- Average outbound shipping: $2.15 per unit (Shopify D2C) ⚠️
- Blended gross margin target: ~52–56% at MSRP (before ads and fulfillment) ⚠️
- AOV target at launch: $24–$28 (bundle with refills) ⚠️

Claims & Compliance
- No parabens, no phthalates, no synthetic dyes
- Not tested on animals
- Compliant with US household cleaning labeling (CPSC), SDS available
- Avoid absolute claims like “non-toxic”; use “safer-choice” phrasing pending certification ⚠️

Competitor Set (for positioning)
- Method, Mrs. Meyer’s, Seventh Generation, Blueland
- Observed gaps: refill convenience and honest scent profiles that avoid overpowering fragrances

Search & Content Inputs
- Seed keywords: “plant based cleaner”, “eco-friendly cleaner”, “refillable cleaner”, “biodegradable cleaner”
- Content pillars: ingredient transparency, practical sustainability, refill economics, scent notes

Visual Direction (initial hypothesis)
- Palette: Leaf green and deep forest accents; neutral off-white base; signal “clean” without medical sterility
- Typography: Humanist sans for body, a friendly serif accent for headlines ⚠️
- Imagery: Real homes, natural light, kitchens and baths with subtle lived-in texture; avoid sterile CGI

Operational Notes
- Shopify: Subscriptions for refills; bundling (starter kit + 2 refills)
- Amazon: FBA for hero SKU; Storefront + A+ Content; subscribe & save (pending supply forecast ⚠️)
- Packaging: Recyclable paperboard outer; glass or aluminum bottle option; QR code links to SDS and refill guide

Asks for the Branding Deliverable
- Positioning statement and messaging hierarchy
- Channel-specific guidance for Amazon and Shopify
- Visual identity guidelines (colors, typography, imagery)
- Prioritized action items with clear next steps for 0–60 days
