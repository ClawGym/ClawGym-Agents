Telemedicine Prenatal Visit Workflow (v1)

Use these step IDs when mapping compliance requirements.

[STEP-1] Appointment scheduling and eligibility screening
- Confirm pregnancy status and estimated due date.
- Verify state of residence and provider licensing coverage.
- Determine if telemedicine is appropriate for the encounter.

[STEP-2] Pre-visit technology check and consent capture
- Send device check link and instructions.
- Provide telemedicine consent for review and signature prior to visit.

[STEP-3] Visit start: patient identity verification
- Verify patient identity (e.g., name, DOB, photo ID if applicable).
- Confirm location at time of visit and emergency contact.

[STEP-4] Clinical intake
- Collect self-reported vitals (e.g., BP if available), symptoms, and standardized questionnaires.
- Update allergies and medication list.

[STEP-5] Clinician encounter: video consult with audio-only fallback
- Conduct the consult via secure video.
- If video fails or is unavailable, proceed via audio-only per policy.

[STEP-6] Orders and e-prescriptions
- Place orders for labs/imaging if needed; send eRx to patient’s pharmacy.

[STEP-7] Documentation and billing
- Document encounter and clinical decisions.
- Apply appropriate telehealth billing and modifiers.

[STEP-8] Follow-up plan and patient reminders
- Provide after-visit summary and next steps.
- Schedule follow-up and patient education reminders.
