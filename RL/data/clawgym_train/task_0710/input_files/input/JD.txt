Senior Data Analyst — Product Analytics (Consumer App)

About the Role:
We are seeking a Senior Data Analyst to partner with Product, Design, and Engineering to drive user activation, retention, and engagement. You will own the product metrics framework, design and evaluate A/B tests, and translate insights into decisions that move the needle on growth.

Responsibilities:
- Define and maintain North Star and guardrail metrics for activation and retention
- Build dashboards and self-serve tools for PMs and designers
- Collaborate with experimentation leads to design, launch, and analyze A/B tests
- Investigate funnel drop-offs and propose actionable improvements
- Lead deep dives on feature adoption and cohort retention
- Communicate insights clearly to stakeholders and influence roadmaps

Qualifications:
- 5+ years in product analytics or growth analytics
- Advanced SQL; proficiency with Python or R preferred
- Experimentation design and causal inference basics
- Experience with event instrumentation and product telemetry
- Ability to frame ambiguous problems and deliver clear recommendations
- Strong communication with non-technical stakeholders

Nice to Have:
- Experience with Amplitude, Looker, or Mode
- Mobile analytics background
- Prior work at a consumer subscription company

Success in 90 Days:
- Establish a product metrics hierarchy for activation/retention
- Ship at least one impactful A/B test improving a key metric
- Build trust with PMs through clear, concise storytelling
