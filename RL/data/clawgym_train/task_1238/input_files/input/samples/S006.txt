Recipe for arrival: Start with garlic smashed under a bowl. Add winter light from the bus stop, thin and shaking. Stir in stories your mother saved in a coffee tin. Salt with two languages and the wages from last week. Simmer while your hands remember factory heat. Taste. It needs brightness—slice scallions into confetti. Serve with cousins, pay stubs, and a joke your uncle tells badly. On the back of the receipt, write: We made it edible.
