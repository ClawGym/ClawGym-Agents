---
title: The Lantern on the Battlements
location: East Battlements
century: 17
tags:
  - signal
  - storm
narrator: Aunt Morag Donnan
---
On winter nights, a lone lantern was said to pace the eastern wall, keeping time with the gusts off the loch.
Aunt Morag would hush us and listen for the chain clinking, claiming it was the watch kept for a ship that never came home.
To this day, when the lake mists rise, a faint amber glow is seen threading the stones.
