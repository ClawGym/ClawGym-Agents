---
title: "Midnight Stagecoach Kicks Up Dust"
outlet: "Western Weekly"
reviewer: "Opal Hart"
date: "1953-09-17"
film: "Midnight Stagecoach (1953)"
---

The film opens with a burst of energy and a harmonica refrain.

Elliot Grant offers a strong presence, but the plot drags by the second act.

Still, the final chase earns its stripes.
