Statement from Northbridge Metals Regarding Riverbend Plant Incident

On April 3 at approximately 2:15 PM local time, an equipment failure triggered a brief fire in the casting bay of our Riverbend Plant. The fire was minor and fully extinguished within minutes. Two contractors on site experienced smoke inhalation and were treated by EMS; there were no serious injuries. We resumed normal operations within 90 minutes.

Our equipment meets all global standards, and our safety culture is unmatched in the industry. Early indications suggest this may have been caused by sabotage, which we take very seriously. We are also confident the air quality posed no risk to nearby residents.

We apologize for any concern and will share more as we learn it.
