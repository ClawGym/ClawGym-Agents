# Hematology – Study Notes

Topic: Anemias
Term: Iron deficiency anemia
Definition: Microcytic anemia characterized by low ferritin and high TIBC; common causes include chronic blood loss.
Mnemonic: TAILS (Thin RBCs, Anisocytosis, Iron low, Low ferritin, Spoon nails)
Links: RBC indices, Microcytosis

Term: Anemia of chronic disease
Definition: Inflammation-induced iron sequestration with normal/high ferritin and low TIBC mediated by hepcidin.
Links: Hepcidin, Ferritin

Term: Thalassemia
Definition: Genetic decrease in globin synthesis leading to microcytosis with normal iron studies.
Links: Microcytosis, Target cells

Topic: Coagulation
Term: Intrinsic pathway factors
Definition: Factors XII, XI, IX, VIII; abnormalities prolong aPTT.

Term: Extrinsic pathway factors
Definition: Factor VII; abnormalities prolong PT.

Q: How does liver disease affect coagulation tests?

Term: Vitamin K-dependent factors
Definition: Factors II, VII, IX, X; synthesis requires vitamin K and is affected by warfarin and liver dysfunction.
Links: Warfarin, Liver

Topic: Hemolysis
Term: Intravascular hemolysis markers
Definition: Hemoglobinuria, decreased haptoglobin, elevated LDH.
Links: Haptoglobin, LDH

Term: Extravascular hemolysis markers
Definition: Splenomegaly and jaundice due to increased unconjugated bilirubin.
Links: Spleen, Bilirubin
