Campus Political Activity and Privacy Guidelines (Excerpt)

- Faculty may discuss political topics for instructional purposes but must not use university resources to endorse candidates, solicit contributions, or direct support to political committees or PACs.
- Public-facing course materials discussing political topics should include a disclaimer: "The views expressed are those of the author and do not represent the University."
- Publishing or distributing student information that identifies them (e.g., names, email addresses) without documented consent is prohibited.
