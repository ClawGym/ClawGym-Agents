# Trail Notes

## Otter Brook Circular
- Habitat: upland heath and gorse.
- RARE: Marsh fritillary
- Reminder: Avoid trampling gorse near grid SX 68xx 83xx.
- Facilities: limited parking; stile needs repair.

## Tytherley Copse
- Habitat: ancient woodland.
- RARE: Stag beetle
- RARE: Glow-worm
- Volunteer idea: add loggery demonstration.

## Seven Sisters Ridge
- Habitat: chalk grassland.
- RARE: Glow-worm
- Wind exposure; plan dusk segment carefully.

## Roaches Path
- Habitat: gritstone edges.
- Note: High brown fritillary occasional; watch bracken cutting schedule.
