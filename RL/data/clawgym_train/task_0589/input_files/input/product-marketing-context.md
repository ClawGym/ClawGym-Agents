# NimbusCRM — Simple CRM for Small Teams

## One-liner
NimbusCRM is a simple, affordable CRM built for small teams that need to get selling fast without admin overhead.

## Positioning & Value Proposition
- Built for SMB sales teams (2–50 users) that want a clean pipeline, fast onboarding, and transparent pricing.
- Focused on simplicity and speed-to-value: most teams complete setup and import in under 60 minutes.
- Opinionated defaults and templates for founder-led sales, agencies, and services businesses.

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- Team size: 5–20 typical, up to 50
- Segments: Agencies, IT/MSPs, professional services, boutique SaaS, local services with outbound/inbound mix
- Roles: Founder/owner, sales lead, first RevOps hire
- Needs: Visual pipeline, email sync, light automation, basic reporting, easy onboarding

## Key Differentiators
- Ease of use: zero-admin setup; guided onboarding and starter templates
- Price-to-value: lower per-seat cost vs legacy CRMs
- Fast migration: CSV/Pipedrive import wizard with field mapping presets
- Support-first: live chat on all paid plans; migration help included up to 25K records
- Transparent packaging: core features included; no mandatory add-on bundle to get started

## Honest Weaknesses
- Advanced, enterprise-grade reporting and forecasting are limited
- No custom objects beyond standard CRM objects (Contacts, Companies, Deals, Activities)
- SSO limited to Google on Business plan (full SSO/SCIM on Enterprise only)
- Fewer native marketing automation features than marketing-first suites

## Features Overview
- Pipelines: Kanban pipelines with stages, SLAs, and probability
- Email: Two-way sync (Gmail/Outlook), email templates, tracking
- Automation: If-this-then-that workflows (move stage, assign owner, create tasks)
- Reporting: Deal velocity, win rate, activity volume, simple dashboards
- Integrations: Slack, Google Workspace, Outlook, Zapier, Stripe (via Zapier), Calendly
- Mobile: iOS/Android apps for pipelines, activities, and notes

## Pricing (Per User, Per Month)
- Free: $0 — up to 2 users, 1 pipeline, 1,000 contacts, basic email sync
- Starter: $8/user/month (monthly billing) — unlimited pipelines, 25,000 contacts, email sync, 5 automations, standard reports
- Business: $16/user/month (monthly billing) — advanced automation (25 workflows), advanced reports, permissions/roles, Google SSO, priority support
- Enterprise: Custom — SSO/SCIM, audit logs, IP allowlist, dedicated CSM
- Annual billing: 20% discount on per-user pricing (optional)

Notes:
- All paid plans include live chat support and access to the migration assistant.
- No required add-on bundles to unlock email sync or basic web forms.

## Migration & Onboarding
- Import wizard: Presets for CSV, Pipedrive, and HubSpot exports
- What transfers from Pipedrive: Contacts, Organizations, Deals, Activities (meetings/calls/tasks), notes; most standard fields map automatically
- What needs attention: Custom fields mapping review, automations and email templates must be recreated
- Timeline: 1–2 hours for small teams (<10 users), 1–3 days for larger imports (10–50 users)
- Support: Live chat + step-by-step guide + optional white-glove help (included up to 25K records)

## Competitors We See Most
- Pipedrive, HubSpot CRM (Starter), Zoho CRM, Close
- Head-to-head theme: “NimbusCRM vs Pipedrive” — we compete on simplicity, onboarding speed, and total cost for SMBs

## Messaging Pillars
1) Simple enough for day-one adoption
2) Affordable for small teams
3) Honest limits (no bait-and-switch add-ons)
4) Fast migration with human help
