# Unions and the Motherhood Penalty for Latina Workers (study_id: S2)

Using administrative payroll microdata, Latina mothers face a motherhood penalty (motherhood_penalty) of 5% relative to Latina non-mothers with similar job tenure. Unionization is associated with a 15% wage premium (union_premium) for Latina workers, with larger gains among lower-wage occupations.

Notes: Outcomes measured as hourly wages; estimates net of industry and metro fixed effects.
