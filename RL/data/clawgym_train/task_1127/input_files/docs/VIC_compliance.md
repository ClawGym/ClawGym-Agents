# VIC Foreign Investor Residential Property Guide (local excerpt)

State: VIC

## Investor Categories
- Non-resident foreign individual: A person not ordinarily resident in Australia.

## FIRB Approval
- FIRB approval is required for non-resident individuals acquiring new residential dwellings (including off-the-plan apartments).
- Established dwellings are not permitted for non-resident buyers.

## Foreign Buyer Surcharges (Rates)
- Foreign purchaser additional duty: 8% of dutiable value on residential property, in addition to standard duty.

## Land Tax Surcharge (Rates)
- Foreign absentee owner surcharge: 4% on applicable land holdings.

## Prohibited Property Types
- Established residential dwellings for non-resident individuals.

## Penalties for Non-Compliance
- Civil penalties up to AUD 70,000 and potential enforcement action.

## Date-based Conditions
- Certain concessions may apply to contracts signed before 2024-07-01. Confirm whether the contract date triggers different rates or concessions.
