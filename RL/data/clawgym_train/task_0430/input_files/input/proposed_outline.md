# Municipal Digital Skills Short Course (Proposed)

- Schedule: Monday and Wednesday mornings, 10:00–12:00, over two weeks (4 sessions total).
- Delivery mode: online-heavy, live demonstrations of government portals.
- Modules:
  1. Digital Literacy Fundamentals (120)
  2. Safe Internet Practices (90)
  3. Government E-Services Portal (90)
  4. Job Search Basics (90)
  5. Spreadsheets Intro (90)
- Venue: Municipal Training Room 1.
- Notes: participants will log in individually to follow along online.
