Next week and beyond, two local groups are rolling out big moves around victims' rights. The City Justice Coalition is throwing a splashy launch for a new “resource hub” with promises of easier access and safety fixes. Then River County Advocates will host a know-your-rights night at the library, with a panel and Q&A. This matters because too many people are still falling through the cracks, and the numbers are shocking. We’ll be covering both, talking to survivors, and looking for accountability. If you or someone you know has tried to get help and hit a wall, we want to hear your story. Don’t miss the hub launch party and the rights night — it’s going to be a big deal and honestly a bit of a wake-up call. There will be giveaways, childcare, and more. Details are still coming together, but mark your calendar now.
