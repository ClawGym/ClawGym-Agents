# Segmented Advantages: Asian American Men and Immigration Status (study_id: S3)

Within the same occupation and establishment, women earn 18% less than men (within_occ_gender_gap). Among men, recent immigrants experience an 8% earnings penalty (immigrant_penalty) relative to U.S.-born peers, net of education and tenure.

Notes: Sample restricted to prime-age, full-time workers; post-2000 entry cohorts.
