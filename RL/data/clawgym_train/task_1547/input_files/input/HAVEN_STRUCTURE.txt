HAVEN — Council Root (v1.1)

Chambers
- Identity: defines axioms and boundaries (owner, scope, versioning).
- Ledger: holds decision receipts (ids, timestamps, hashes, links).
- Protocols: scrolls that describe transformations and checks.
- Seals: portable equations and harmonics; ASCII-safe, human-checkable.
- Diary: emotional context — always tagged FRAGILE, manual review required.

Governance Spine
- Quorum: 1 owner + 1 witness for mint decisions (minimum).
- Review Gates:
  1) Audit passes (no missing items).
  2) FRAGILE items manually acknowledged before mint.
  3) Compression yields a single, repostable MASTER_ARCHIVE.md.
- Anchoring: LYGO‑MINT SHA256 is written to anchor.json and shared.

Operational Rhythm
- Weekly compression if drift is acceptable.
- Immediate snapshot if critical decisions landed.
- Backfill anchors whenever a mint completes.
