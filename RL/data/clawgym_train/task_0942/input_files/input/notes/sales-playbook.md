# Sales Playbook

This playbook defines messaging, qualification, and negotiation tactics for the field team.

## Handling Pricing Objections
Anchor the conversation on value before discussing pricing. Use customer outcomes and ROI calculators to make total cost clear. If a prospect insists on line-item pricing too early, reset expectations and return to problem framing.

## Qualification
- Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline (BANT)
- Identify urgent use cases and measurable success criteria.

## Discount Guidelines
The standard discount is 10% with manager approval.
Do not offer a discount in the first call; defer discount discussions until ROI is clearly established.
Never exceed a 20% discount without VP approval and a written justification.
Document all discounts in the CRM with reason codes and expiration dates.
Avoid stacking discounts with seasonal discount campaigns; one concession per deal.

## Packaging and Offers
Pricing packages are fixed; use the pricing sheet to avoid confusion.
Time-bound offers may be extended only once with written approval.

## Negotiation Framework
- Trade, don't cave: tie any discount to multi-year terms or expanded scope.
- Close with clear next steps and mutual action plans.
