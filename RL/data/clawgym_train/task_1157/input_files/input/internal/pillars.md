# Talent Brand Pillars

Our hiring message emphasizes candidates with both technical skills and strong leadership abilities.

## Technical Excellence with Ownership
- Deep systems thinking tied to customer outcomes
- Decision records and architectural rigor
- Quality as a leadership behavior

## Leaderful Culture
- Mentorship as a first-class responsibility
- Psychological safety in engineering teams
- Leading without title: initiative and stewardship

## Cross-functional Communication
- Business context in design proposals
- Clear writing and async updates
- Partnering with Product and GTM
