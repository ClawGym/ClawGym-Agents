Candid Conversation

September 15, 2021
Q: What keeps you speaking out?
A: Freedom to ask tough questions and the belief that reason can bridge divides.
Q: Do you think morality depends on religion?
A: No; morality grows from empathy and evidence, not from religion alone.
Q: Where does science fit into your art?
A: Science gives me methods; faith gives some people comfort, but I lean on inquiry.
