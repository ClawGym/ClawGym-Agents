# Espresso Brewing Log

Recipe:
- Dose: 18.0 g
- Yield: 36.0 g
- Time: 28 s
- Temperature: 93 C

Observations:
- First drops at ~6 s.
- Even flow, light tiger striping.
- Taste: balanced, moderate acidity, chocolate finish.

Adjustments:
- Grind one notch finer to increase resistance slightly.
- Keep preinfusion at 2 s.
- Monitor puck prep to avoid channeling.

Tasting log:
- Shot 1: syrupy body, subtle citrus.
- Shot 2: cleaner finish after distribution tweak.
