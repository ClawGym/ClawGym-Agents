# Pricing FAQ

This Pricing FAQ explains our pricing in simple terms for customers evaluating plans.

## How does your pricing work?
Our pricing is tiered: Starter, Growth, and Business. Each tier includes a defined usage allowance and support level.

## Do you offer a discount for nonprofits?
Yes, we provide a 20% discount for verified nonprofits and a 10% discount for students where applicable.

## Any annual prepay discount or bulk discount?
Annual billing effectively includes two months free. We also evaluate bulk discount requests for large deployments.

## Where can I see pricing details?
See the pricing page for examples, feature comparison, and a pricing calculator to estimate expected spend.

## Do discount codes stack with promotional discount?
No. Discount codes cannot be combined with any other promotional discount. The higher-value discount applies.

## Are there hidden fees in the pricing?
No. Our pricing is transparent: no setup fees, overage penalties, or support surcharges.

## Can I change plans mid-cycle and how does pricing adjust?
You can upgrade or downgrade anytime; pricing is pro-rated to the day.

## Is there custom pricing for enterprise deals?
Yes. Contact sales for enterprise pricing, procurement documentation, and security reviews.
