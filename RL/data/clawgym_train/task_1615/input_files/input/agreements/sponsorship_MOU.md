# Community Orchestra – Riverton High School Football Program Sponsorship MOU

Date: 2026-08-15

1. Purpose
The River City Community Orchestra ("Orchestra") and the Riverton High School Football Program ("School") agree to collaborate on a halftime performance to promote community unity and student engagement.

2. Branding and Trademarks
Neither party will use professional league names (e.g., "NFL", "National Football League") or event names (e.g., "Super Bowl"), or any third-party logos in publicity for the event. References to the high school team nickname "Riverton Eagles" are permitted consistent with school policy and without implying external endorsement.

3. Youth Protection and Supervision
All volunteers with direct contact with minors must have a cleared background check on file with the District. A minimum ratio of one cleared adult chaperone per ten students will be maintained. No photographs of minors may be published without prior parent or guardian consent as required by District policy.

4. Alcohol and Safety
Alcohol is prohibited on school grounds and at school-sponsored events. Performers under 18 must be supervised at all times by designated chaperones.

5. Liability and Insurance
Each party will maintain general liability insurance appropriate for school events. Participants will sign a waiver of liability for non-negligent incidents, and both parties agree to coordinate safety practices in good faith.
