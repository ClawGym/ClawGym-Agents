# Benefits and Support

## Mental Health Support
All employees have access to confidential counseling via the Employee Assistance Program (EAP), up to six sessions per issue at no cost.

## COBRA Coverage
Medical, dental, and vision coverage can be continued under COBRA for up to 18 months. Enrollment information is sent within 14 days of your termination date.

## Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
Provides short-term counseling, referral services, and crisis support. Available 24/7 at 1-800-000-0000.
