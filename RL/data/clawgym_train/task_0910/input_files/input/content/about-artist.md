# About the Artist
I work with paper, textiles, wood, and found materials, often incorporating fragments from my collection of old volumes. My practice balances structure and improvisation; I document shifts in materials and methods in my [studio notes](/studio-exhibitions) when it helps clarify intent for viewers and collaborators.

I value durable craft and quiet surfaces. When possible, I source components locally or reuse what I already have on hand. The library of worn titles beside my workbench remains a steady well of texture and typography.
