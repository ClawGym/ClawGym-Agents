# Vintage Finds: Rare Titles
I catalogue interesting volumes that pass through my hands—bindings with character, odd formats, and quietly designed covers. The selection changes as I make space on the shelves; when something needs a gentle mend I note it in [repair notes](/book-repair-notes).

My interests lean toward mid-century design, slim poetry chapbooks, and small-press catalogs. I share occasional photos to give a sense of scale and patina.
