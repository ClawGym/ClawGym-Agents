# Notes on Repairs
These are informal notes on light conservation I do for my own library: hinging loose plates, mending small tears, and tightening text blocks when safe. When in doubt I leave things as found and add a sleeve. For related context on why I keep certain volumes, see [vintage finds](/vintage-rare-finds).

I keep materials simple and reversible where possible. Most entries are reminders to myself about adhesives, tissue weights, and failures worth avoiding.
