# Project: AcmeCo Website CRO Overhaul
Project Slug: acmeco-website-cro-overhaul

What: Overhaul AcmeCo’s homepage and top three landing pages to increase qualified lead conversions by 35% through a structured CRO program.
Why: Paid traffic is underperforming with a 1.2% visitor-to-lead rate; messaging is unclear, social proof is buried, and CTAs lack hierarchy — leadership needs measurable uplift before Q3.

Done Criteria:
- Homepage and top three landing pages updated, reviewed, and deployed to production
- Primary KPI (visitor-to-lead rate) improves by at least +35% relative to baseline over a 14-day post-launch window
- GA4 events and Looker Studio dashboard tracking primary and secondary CTAs are implemented and validated
- At least two A/B tests (headline and CTA) run to statistical significance or for a minimum 14-day window with documented results
- Client sign-off on final copy, wireframes, and launched pages
- Delivery of a written Results Report with next-step recommendations
