# Dopamine Menu (rough draft)

These are things that help me get moving or reset my brain when I start to stall.

## Quick hits
- Stretch arms and neck, breathe for a minute
- One-song dance break
- Pet the dog, sip cold water
- Look out the window and find 3 green things
- Delete 5 emails or archive a small batch
- Fidget cube for a couple minutes

## Medium activities
- 10–15 min walk outside (sunlight if possible)
- Short yoga/stretch video on YouTube
- Journal a few lines about “What’s the next tiny step?”
- Clean one surface (desk, counter, nightstand)
- Call or voice memo a friend
- Update a playlist

## Bigger resets
- 30–45 min workout (bodyweight circuit or bike)
- Cook a simple meal I like
- Work on a small creative project (sketch, music, photo edits)
- Read a chapter of a book or listen to a podcast episode
- Play one round of a game
- Plan a weekend mini-adventure

## Treats / special
- Movie night
- Museum visit or new café
- Longer hike or bike ride
- Concert or local event
- Board game night with friends

Notes:
- Pair boring tasks with audio (podcast/playlist) when it won’t break focus.
- Keep some options available at home without much setup.
- Use timers so resets don’t sprawl.
