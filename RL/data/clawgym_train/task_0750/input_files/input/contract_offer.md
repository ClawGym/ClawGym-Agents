Record Deal Offer Summary
Offer Version: v1.0 (2026-03-10)

Key Economic Terms:
- Advance: $75,000 total, payable $25,000 on signing, $25,000 on delivery, $25,000 on release. Fully recoupable.
- Royalty Rate: 18% of Net Receipts on streaming and downloads.
- Royalty Escalator: Increases to 20% after cumulative 150,000,000 streams across the album’s primary releases.
- Net Receipts Definition (for streaming/downloads): Gross platform payouts less the distributor fee (20%) and applicable taxes/withholding.
- Distributor Fee: 20% of gross platform payouts (deducted before Net Receipts).
- Recoupment Scope: Advances plus approved marketing spend, recoupable from artist royalties. Marketing spend recoupment is capped at $30,000.

Term & Misc:
- Territory: World.
- Term: One (1) album, plus two (2) option periods at label’s election.
- Accounting: Semi-annual statements and payments.
- No packaging deductions or breakage on digital formats.

Notes:
- Any unapproved marketing spend is not recoupable.
- Escalator applies prospectively once the threshold is crossed.
