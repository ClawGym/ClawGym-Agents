Client constraints and preferences

- Budget: strict budget cap of 200k EUR for the first year; we prefer to deliver an MVP within six months (target 6 months).
- Team: two technical founders plus one part-time PM. Strengths: Python, MLOps, and B2B SaaS. Weaknesses: limited medical device and clinical QA experience.
- Region and customers: start in the EU, initial focus on DACH and Nordics; primary target is SMB/SME customers with simple onboarding and self-serve paths.
- Compliance and data: avoid regulated data when we can. If we must touch it, enforce privacy-by-design (edge/on-device), minimize PII, and avoid storing images or biometric data. Must be GDPR-ready. Medical/CE routes add timeline risk we want to minimize.
- Go-to-market: bottom-up self-serve with light sales assistance. Ideal price band is 100–500 EUR/month per customer. Require integrations with common SMB tools (e.g., Xero, QuickBooks, Microsoft 365/SharePoint).
- Infrastructure: cloud-first; BYOK optional for larger accounts. Avoid bespoke hardware or site installs that require building access or utility approvals.
- Timeline: we need a credible pilot in 6 months and early revenue within 12 months.
- Investor context: we plan to approach EU VCs active in B2B SaaS, climate/energy efficiency, and compliance tooling. Visible investor activity in the space is a plus.
- Risk tolerance: moderate. Preference for repeatable SaaS revenue, shorter sales cycles, and low regulatory burden.
- Dependencies to avoid: long utility partnership cycles, hospital approvals, or any on-prem deployments that would delay learning cycles.
