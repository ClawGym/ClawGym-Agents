# Layoff Policy

## Severance
Employees who are laid off will receive two weeks of base pay for every full year of service, with a minimum of four weeks and a maximum of twenty-four weeks.
Payout is provided as a lump-sum within 30 days of termination.

## Notice Period
Where feasible, the company provides at least two weeks' notice. If notice is not feasible, pay in lieu of notice is provided.

## Eligibility
Regular full-time employees employed for at least six months are eligible for severance. Contractors and interns are not eligible.
