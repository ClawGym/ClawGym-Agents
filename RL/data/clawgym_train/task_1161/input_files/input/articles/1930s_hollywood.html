1930s Hollywood Soft Glam

Era: 1930s

The classic 1930s look emphasizes thin brows, soft shadow, and a satin matte complexion.

Materials

  • Cold cream
  • Cake mascara (black)
  • Pressed powder (ivory)
  • Neutral eyeshadow (taupe)
  • Lipstick (deep raspberry)

Steps

  1. Cleanse with cold cream and wipe away excess.
  2. Apply pressed powder for a satin-matte base.
  3. Define thin brows with pencil, keeping a soft arch.
  4. Sweep taupe shadow on lids and blend softly.
  5. Apply cake mascara to upper lashes only.
  6. Finish with a precise raspberry lip.

Cautions

  • Do not over-darken brows; the decade favors a delicate line.
  • Test cake mascara for sensitivity before use.
