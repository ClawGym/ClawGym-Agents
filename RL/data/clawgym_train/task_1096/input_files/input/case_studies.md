# Case Studies — Selected Outcomes

## Retail (Omnichannel)
Client: FashionHub (mid-market apparel)
Challenge: Fragmented store and online inventory leading to stockouts and split carts.
Solution: Implemented unified order routing and inventory visibility across DC, store, and dropship.
Outcome:
- Checkout conversion increased by 27%
- Average order value up 18%
- Store fulfillment time reduced by 22%

## Manufacturing (IoT & Predictive Maintenance)
Client: NovaFab Manufacturing
Challenge: Unplanned downtime on CNC lines and lack of telemetry standardization.
Solution: Edge gateways + IoT platform with anomaly detection and OEE dashboards.
Outcome:
- Unplanned downtime reduced by 31%
- Maintenance labor hours down 12%
- First-fix rate improved by 9%

## Logistics (TMS Integration)
Client: NorthStar Logistics (3PL)
Challenge: Disconnected TMS and WMS causing manual replans and delays.
Solution: API-based integration with event-driven status updates and exception workflows.
Outcome:
- Dock-to-dock cycle time improved by 23%
- On-time delivery improved by 15%
- Manual exception handling reduced by 28%
