# Universal Miniature Painting Guide

## Prep
- Lightly wipe parts with a dry cloth. Washing is optional for most figures.
- Remove sprue with a hobby knife; a quick scrape is fine.

## Glue
- Plastic cement works well for most joints, even on metal figures; super glue is optional.
- Clamps are usually unnecessary if you hold parts for 30 seconds.

## Priming
- Use any acrylic primer; one or two heavy coats will cover quickly.
- Start painting after about one hour of dry time. Works fine for plastic and metal.

## Painting
- Begin with a thick base coat to establish coverage fast.
- Add highlights with a single lighter pass.

## Safety
- Spray outdoors or crack a window for airflow.

## Tools
- Hobby knife, primer, and paint set.
