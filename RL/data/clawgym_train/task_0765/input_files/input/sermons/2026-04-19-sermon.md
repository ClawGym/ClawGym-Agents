On the Sea of Galilee, the disciples faced a storm that tested them. Jesus slept as waves rose; when He spoke, the winds obeyed. In this message, we will consider the fears that rattle us and the faith that steadies us.

Reflection questions:
- What does trust look like when circumstances shout louder than hope?
- How does the presence of Christ change our posture in chaos?
