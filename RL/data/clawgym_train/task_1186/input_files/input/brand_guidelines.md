Brand voice: friendly, supportive, clear. Audience: students and casual football fans.

Tone rules:
- Be energetic but not hypey. Use simple English.
- Keep sentences short and direct.
- Invite engagement with a clear call-to-action (CTA).
- Use football terms correctly (match, training, derby, analysis).
- Avoid slang and culture-specific idioms.
- Keep player and team names exactly as given.
