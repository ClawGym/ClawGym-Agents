# Content Planning Notes — HFictionKid

Goal:
- Promote a summer reading list and a virtual Family Discussion Night (free, online, 7pm; last weekend next month).
- Grow engagement by prompting thoughtful, family-friendly questions.

Posting rhythm & channels:
- 2 posts per week (mix Instagram + TikTok).
- Keep captions under 200 words; avoid spoilers.
- Aim for varied historical periods across the month.

Voice & tone:
- Curious, warm, and inclusive. Think: a thoughtful teen who asks genuine questions at dinner.
- Invite multiple viewpoints respectfully.

Hook starters (begin hooks with one of these):
- What if...
- Would you rather...
- One-minute timeline:
- Hidden clue:

CTA options (use exactly as written):
- Comment your question for family night
- Save this for family night
- DM me "List" for the PDF summer reading list
- Share this with someone who likes history

Content ideas to prioritize:
- Family discussion prompts linked to book themes
- Short explainers that make timelines feel intuitive
- Visual or sensory details from settings

Other notes:
- Encourage participation regardless of prior knowledge.
- Keep it spoiler-light but thought-provoking.
