# City Heat Emergency Protocol (v1.1)

## Scope
This protocol applies to heat events affecting city residents and visitors.

## Activation Triggers
- National Weather Service issues a heat advisory for 2 consecutive days.
- Heat index >= 100°F in any borough.

## Protective Actions
- Open cooling centers daily 09:00–19:00.
- Coordinate hydration stations in high-traffic areas.
- Conduct wellness checks on seniors and homebound residents.

## Communications
- Send SMS notifications to registered residents by 08:00 on activation days.
- Post website updates within 2 hours of trigger confirmation and amplify via social media.
