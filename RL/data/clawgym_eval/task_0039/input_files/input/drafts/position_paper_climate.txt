Climate Policy — Draft (Aronia)

We support a carbon tax to internalize externalities and send a clear price signal that guides investment toward low-emission technologies.

We oppose cap-and-trade at this stage due to market volatility concerns and measurement uncertainties in permit allocation.

A targeted subsidy for clean R&D can complement a carbon tax without locking in long-run inefficiencies, provided awards are competitively allocated and sunset clauses are enforced.
