# DRAFT 1 — HONEY MASK
On long shoot days my skin gets parched, and between retakes I need something simple. Lately I’ve been using a raw honey mask after we wrap: I mix a teaspoon with a little warm water and smooth it on for 10 minutes. It supposedly cures breakouts fast and heals redness, and honestly it feels like a miracle on set. I love how soft it leaves me, and I feel camera-ready again.

# DRAFT 2 — GREEN TEA TONER
My travel kit always hides a DIY green tea toner. I steep two bags, let it cool, then swipe it on with reusable cotton. It erases fine lines, reverses dullness, and gives a guaranteed glow before interviews. I like how lightweight it feels under makeup and it’s so easy to make in a hotel room.

# DRAFT 3 — OATMEAL CLEANSE
For mornings when my face needs extra love, I stir oats with warm water into a gentle scrubby cleanse. The texture is a fix for everything, from acne to irritation, and it makes me feel clean but not tight. It’s quick, cheap, and I can throw it together while memorizing lines.
