Springfield Municipal Code - Title 7: Animals

Sec. 7-12. Dogs at large prohibited.

It shall be unlawful for any person to permit a dog to run at large within the city.

All dogs must be restrained by a leash not exceeding six (6) feet in length when on public sidewalks and parks.

Penalty: A violation of this section is punishable by a fine not to exceed $100 for a first offense.
