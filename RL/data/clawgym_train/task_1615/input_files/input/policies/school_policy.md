# Riverton School District Game Day Event Policy (Excerpt)

Youth Protection
All volunteers must be cleared through the District process; minors must be supervised by cleared adults at all times and one-to-one unsupervised interactions are not permitted.

Photo/Media
Obtain documented consent from parents/guardians before publishing identifiable images of minors on public channels.

Trademarks and Endorsements
Do not use third-party trademarks (including "Super Bowl", "NFL", or "National Football League") in a manner that implies endorsement or partnership. When in doubt, use generic terms such as "championship game" or "pro football". Include a disclaimer for community group participation: "This event is independent and not affiliated with any professional league or organization."

Alcohol and Substances
Alcohol is prohibited on all district property and at all district events, including practices, games, and community collaborations.

School Marks
Use of school marks (name, logo, mascot) is permitted with prior Principal approval and must conform to style guidelines.
