# QLD Foreign Investor Residential Property Guide (local excerpt)

State: QLD

## Investor Categories
- Non-resident foreign individual: A person not ordinarily resident in Australia.

## FIRB Approval
- FIRB approval generally required for non-resident purchases of new residential property.

## Foreign Buyer Surcharges (Rates)
- Foreign purchaser duty surcharge: 7% on residential property, in addition to standard duty.

## Land Tax Surcharge (Rates)
- Land tax surcharge for foreign persons: 2% (where applicable).

## Prohibited Property Types
- Established residential dwellings for non-resident individuals.

## Date-based Conditions
- Rates may vary over time; confirm the applicable rates for the intended contract date.
