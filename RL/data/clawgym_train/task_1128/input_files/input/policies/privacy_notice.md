# Neighborhood Association Privacy Notice (Excerpts)

## Allowed data collection
- name (required)
- email (optional)
- phone (not permitted)

## Consent
- Forms must include an unchecked checkbox with the label "I consent to receive event updates by email."

## Retention
- Delete attendee contact information within 30 days after the event.

## Sharing
- We do not sell or share personal data.

## Required disclosures on forms
- A link labeled "Privacy Notice" pointing to the association's notice.
- A clear cancellation policy statement.
- An accessibility notice with contact details.
- Opt-out instruction: "You can unsubscribe at any time."
