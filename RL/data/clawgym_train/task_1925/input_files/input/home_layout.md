Home Layout — Two-Story Townhouse (Austin, TX 78704)
Summary
- Orientation: Front door faces south toward shared driveway/visitor parking; back sliding door opens north to a small fenced patio and gate to the alley.
- Structure: Two stories, bedrooms upstairs; no basement.
- Stairs: 14 steps, handrail on right when going up from ground floor; carpeted; under-stair half-bath.
- Flooring: Tile in kitchen/entry, carpet in living and upstairs hall/bedrooms.
- Windows: All bedroom windows are sliding and operable; insect screens can be removed by pulling tabs.

Ground Floor (Level 1)
- Entry hall: Front door (solid core, inward-swing), deadbolt. Secondary exit via back sliding glass door to patio gate.
- Living room: Large front-facing window (south). Interior door to half-bath under stairs.
- Kitchen/Dining: Back sliding door (north) to patio; windows above sink (east-facing).
- Half-bath under stairs: Interior room with no windows; potential tornado shelter space (reinforced by surrounding walls).
- Garage/Carport: Single car space at front; interior door into kitchen hall (kept unlocked when home).
- Fire extinguisher: 1) Under kitchen sink, 2) In garage by door to kitchen.
- Circuit breaker panel: In garage, right wall near interior door (labeled for major circuits).
- Gas appliances: Gas water heater in garage closet; gas range in kitchen (shutoff at appliance + meter outside).
- Smoke/CO detectors (existing): 1 on the ceiling near stair bottom; 1 in kitchen hallway (10-year sealed battery).

Upper Floor (Level 2)
- Primary bedroom (Nora & Luis): South-facing windows above driveway (two sliding windows). Door to hall; closet. Potential egress: door → hall → stairs → front door; or window exit with escape ladder to front driveway.
- Bedroom 2 (Eli): North-facing window over patio (one operable sliding window). Egress: door → hall → stairs → front door; or window to backyard with escape ladder to patio.
- Bedroom 3 (Abuela Rosa): North-facing window over patio (one operable sliding window), closest to stairs. Egress: door → hall → stairs → front door; or window to backyard with escape ladder to patio. Extra space for cane access beside bed.
- Full bathroom: Interior room; no exterior windows.
- Hallway: Linen closet; attic hatch.
- Smoke detectors (existing): In hallway outside bedrooms; in each bedroom ceiling (battery units installed 2020; monthly test needed).
- Carbon monoxide detector: Plug-in unit in upstairs hall near bedrooms.

Exits by Room (Two Exits Each)
- Primary bedroom: (1) Door to hall → stairs → front door; (2) South window to driveway — requires 2-story escape ladder.
- Eli’s bedroom: (1) Door to hall → stairs → front door; (2) North window to patio — requires 2-story escape ladder.
- Rosa’s bedroom: (1) Door to hall → stairs → front door (assign helper on stairs); (2) North window to patio — requires 2-story escape ladder.

Yard/Exterior
- Patio: Small fenced area with gate latch operable from inside; clear pathway to alley.
- Gas meter: East exterior wall near kitchen (ground level; quarter-turn shutoff).
- Water shutoff: Curb box in front near driveway; secondary ball valve where main line enters garage.
- Electric service: Exterior feed to garage; main panel in garage (see above).

Notes & Condition Checks
- All bedroom windows open fully without tools; screens can be removed by pulling tabs.
- Keep two 2-story escape ladders stored upstairs (see plan’s ladder placement).
- Night lighting: Plug-in nightlights in upstairs hall and stair landing for visibility during outages.
- Pets: Cat (Luna) typically stays upstairs during the day; carrier is stored in hall closet for quick evacuation.
