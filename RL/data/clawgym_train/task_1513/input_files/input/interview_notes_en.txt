Project: Coastal District Cash and DRR Pilot (Phase II)

== Key Findings ==
- Community leaders reported that cash assistance improved flexibility but was hindered by poor mobile coverage for e-wallet disbursements. [Q1]
- Participants highlighted gaps in accountability and limited awareness of available grievance mechanisms. [Q2]
- Households using temporary shelter farther from markets faced higher transport costs for basic goods. [Q3]

== Recommendations ==
- Integrate disaster risk reduction (DRR) training with cash distributions to build community resilience. [Q4]
- Strengthen grievance mechanisms and feedback loops in all cash programs. [Q5]
- Coordinate with municipal shelter committees to improve temporary shelter options near markets. [Q6]
- Phase livelihoods support alongside cash to avoid dependency and encourage local enterprise. [Q7]
