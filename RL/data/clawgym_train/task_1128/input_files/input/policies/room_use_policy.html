Community Center Room Use Policy (Excerpt)

Event Host Obligations

  • Disclose data collection practices on any sign-up form or webpage used for events.
  • Provide a clear cancellation policy statement.
  • Include an accessibility notice with contact details.
  • Do not collect more than name and email without explicit consent.
  • Keep attendee contact information no longer than 30 days after the event.

Noise and Setup

Amplified sound is not allowed. Return room to original condition after use.
