Board Access & Sharing Preferences

Objectives
- Protect sensitive commercial and employee information while enabling efficient diligence.
- Align with investor preference for watermarking and limited downloads on sensitive contracts.

Access Tiers
1) Pre-NDA (Teaser)
   - Contents: summary deck, high-level metrics (MRR, ARR, growth, gross margin), select logos, market overview, product one-pager.
   - Restrictions: viewable without login download; no underlying customer or employee data.

2) Post-NDA (Data Room)
   - Contents: full folder structure across Corporate, Financial, Legal, Commercial, Product & Tech, HR & Team, and Compliance.
   - Restrictions: view-only with user-specific watermarking on all sensitive docs; downloads disabled for:
     - Customer contracts, pricing schedules, and discount approvals
     - Employee-level data (compensation, option grants with PII)
     - Source code excerpts and security architecture details
   - Allowed controlled downloads (watermarked): financial model spreadsheets, ARR waterfall, and anonymized cohort metrics.

3) Management-only
   - Contents: detailed cap table with emails, raw security logs, incident postmortems, salary-level detail, source code, internal Jira.
   - Restrictions: no downloads; screen capture deterrence via watermarking; access limited to lead investor diligence teams upon request.

Operational Notes
- Time-bounded access: default 30 days from NDA execution.
- Role-based: legal counsel read-only; lead investor may request temporary export of financial model (watermarked).
- All exports must be logged and watermarked with user, timestamp, and IP.
- Redact PII wherever feasible before sharing (especially HR & Team and Commercial exhibits).
