Title: W — Profile, Story, and Trivia (English Notes)

File:
- A Sarkaz mercenary known only as “W,” notorious for planting traps and remote explosives with theatrical flair.
- Former Reunion operative; now operates independently and occasionally collaborates with Rhodes Island under uneasy terms.
- Exhibits a volatile yet calculating personality; playfully sadistic banter masks a survivor’s instinct and sharp tactical mind.

Story:
- In urban combat theaters, W leverages delayed explosives and misdirection to fracture enemy lines, creating chaos before precise strikes.
- Her history with Reunion left her familiar with guerrilla warfare and psychological pressure; she exploits fear, timing, and terrain.
- When dealing with Rhodes Island, she oscillates between provocation and cooperation, often testing boundaries while honoring practical agreements.

Trivia:
- Prefers detonators she can toy with during conversation; it keeps others guessing.
- Known to needle leaders to expose weaknesses, but rarely crosses lines that would burn mutually beneficial bridges.
- Her codename being a single letter reflects a life lived behind masks and misdirection.

Combat/Archetype Notes:
- Role: Long-range area denial and burst damage via explosives.
- Tactics: Set-and-spring ambushes, staggered detonations, and baiting maneuvers.
- Weaknesses: Overconfidence and thrill-seeking can complicate team coordination.
