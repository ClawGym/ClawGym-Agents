# RelayFlow — Product Overview

Product name: RelayFlow  
Category: Customer onboarding and adoption playbook automation (lightweight Customer Success platform)

Summary
RelayFlow helps Customer Success teams at growing B2B SaaS companies turn messy onboarding into repeatable, usage-triggered playbooks that accelerate time-to-value and reduce early churn. It connects sales handoff, CS tasks, product usage signals, and customer-facing checklists into one simple flow.

The Core 5 Questions

1) WHO is this for?
- Roles: Head of Customer Success, Customer Success Manager (CSM), Implementation Lead
- Company type: B2B SaaS
- Company stage/size: 10–100 employees (Series A–B), CS team of 2–8
- Current hacks: Spreadsheets + generic PM tools (Boardly/Trello equivalents), manual email nudges, inconsistent sales → CS handoff notes in CRM

2) WHAT problem do we solve?
- Pain: Onboarding is inconsistent and slow; customers stall before first value. Hand-offs from sales to CS are error-prone. CSMs chase usage manually and miss at-risk accounts.
- Trigger: Adding 2nd–5th CSM; churn creeping up in first 90 days; founders step back from CS; need to standardize onboarding playbooks.
- Cost of doing nothing: Slower activation, higher early churn, overworked CSMs, lost expansions.

3) HOW do we solve it?
- Approach: Pre-built, CS-focused onboarding playbooks that auto-trigger tasks and customer nudges from real product usage (via Segment/Amplitude webhook or direct API). Live customer checklists keep buyers aligned. Two-way integrations with HubSpot and Stripe match contracts and renewal milestones.
- Why it works: Purpose-built for CS, so playbooks map to handoffs, milestones, and usage events—no complex admin. Teams get “TTV” and “90-day activation” dashboards out-of-the-box.
- Stickiness: Playbooks become the operating system for onboarding; templates and usage triggers codify institutional knowledge.

4) WHY is this better?
- Alternatives: Generic project tools (Boardly, NoteDock), spreadsheets (SheetOps), lightweight CS suites (GrowthSight Core, VitalTrack, CatalyzeCS) that require heavy config and admin.
- Unfair advantage: 1-day setup with prebuilt SaaS onboarding templates and direct usage-trigger automation—no admin headcount required.
- “Only we” statement: Only we ship usage-triggered CS playbooks in under a day because we combine native HubSpot/Stripe integrations with no-code event listeners and customer-facing checklists.

5) SO WHAT?
- Transformation: Consistent onboarding, faster time-to-first-value, fewer “silent” accounts, and clearer CS capacity planning.
- Outcomes (pilot data): Across 12 pilot teams, RelayFlow reduced average onboarding cycle time by 35%, increased 90-day activation by 18%, and reduced early churn by 9% in SMB accounts.

Go-to-Market Context
- Motion: Founder-led sales + PLG trial (14 days); best inbound from HubSpot app directory and SaaS peer referrals.
- ICP performance: Strongest conversion with B2B SaaS (10–100 employees) using HubSpot and Stripe; weaker with 100–500 employees preferring full CS suites and custom reporting.
- Pricing: $79 per seat/month; starter plan includes 3 playbooks and core integrations.

Notable Use Case
- “Syncly” (35-employee B2B SaaS): Standardized onboarding via three playbooks tied to product usage milestones; saw 42% reduction in onboarding time and a 23% increase in 90-day feature adoption.

Positioning Clarity Notes
- We win when: CS team is small/lean, wants fast setup, prioritizes onboarding and early adoption over deep health scoring/BI.
- We lose when: Org needs complex reporting, multi-product hierarchies, or 200+ employee change management.
