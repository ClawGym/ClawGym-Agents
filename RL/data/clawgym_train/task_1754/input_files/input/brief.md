# Project Brief: Guided by the Night Sky

Working title: Guided by the Night Sky
Role: Film student (director/producer)
Goal: Plan a visually engaging 6-minute short documentary introducing viewers to stars and planets that are practical to film without a star tracker.

Filming assumptions:
- Filming hemisphere: Northern
- Production window months: August, September, October
- Available equipment: DSLR, fast prime lens, tripod (no star tracker)

Creative focus (preferred themes):
- origins
- navigation
- time-lapse

Practical constraints:
- Brightness: prefer targets with brightness_mag <= 6.5
- Scene feasibility: avoid scenes that require a tracking mount; keep scene complexity at 2 or below

Output needs:
- A shortlist of up to 6 targets, prioritized by a simple scoring method that balances rarity, brightness, visibility in the window, and alignment with the preferred themes
- A concise production update I can send to my advisor summarizing what was selected, why, any gaps in theme coverage, risks, and next steps
