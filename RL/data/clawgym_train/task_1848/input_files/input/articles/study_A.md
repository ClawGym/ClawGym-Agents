# Intersecting Penalties: Black Women in the Tech Sector (study_id: S1)

This study analyzes wage disparities among Black women employed in the U.S. tech sector. After controlling for education, experience, and region, Black women earn 12% less than White women (bw_vs_ww) and 22% less than White men (bw_vs_wm). These penalties persist across firm sizes and job families.

Additional notes: The analysis focuses on full-time employees ages 25–54.
