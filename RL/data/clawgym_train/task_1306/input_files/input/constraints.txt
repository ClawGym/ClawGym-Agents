Acme Notifier — Delivery Constraints and Policies

1) Deployment freezes and blackout windows
   - Q3 Feature Freeze: No production deploys Sep 23–Sep 30 (critical patches only).
   - Black Friday Stability Window: No schema changes or risky deploys Nov 22–Nov 30.
   - Year-End Code Freeze: Dec 15–Jan 2 (no production deploys).

2) Staging and soak requirements
   - Minimum staging soak: 10 business days before any production release.
   - EU-related changes must roll out to EU staging at least 14 days before EU production.
   - Security review required 7 days before staging for any compliance/security-tagged feature.

3) Observability and guardrails
   - No alerting features may go live until telemetry has been running in staging for at least 7 days.
   - All new delivery channels must include retry/backoff and idempotency safeguards.
   - A rollback plan (<= 24 hours) is required for features with production blast radius.

4) Launch risk policy
   - Beta Launch Week (Aug 12–Aug 16): Permit low-risk changes only; defer high-risk operations.
   - Marketing Launch Week (Nov 4–Nov 8): Limit changes to UI/content and bug fixes; avoid infra migrations.

5) Regional and data-residency requirements
   - EU data residency: All European customer data must remain within EU infrastructure.
   - Regional failover must be disabled by default and enabled only after observability and playbooks are in place.

6) Testing and promotion
   - Require staging test evidence and sign-off from Feature Owner and QA before promotion.
   - For dependency chains, upstream components must be GA before enabling downstream features in production.
