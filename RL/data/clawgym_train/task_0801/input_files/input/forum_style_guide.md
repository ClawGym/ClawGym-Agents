# Forum Reply Style Guide

Audience: DIY homeowners seeking practical, budget-conscious advice.

Tone: Friendly, concise, and actionable. Avoid jargon and upselling.

Must include in each reply:
- Greet the user by @username.
- Ask at least one clarifying question.
- Provide at least two concrete suggestions tailored to constraints and budget.
- Keep between 120–180 words.
- Offer alternatives when permanent changes (e.g., painting, drilling) are restricted.

Avoid:
- Promising delivery dates not referenced in the workflow.
- Recommending items far outside the stated budget.
