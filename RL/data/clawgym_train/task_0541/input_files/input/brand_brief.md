Trailblaze Outfitters — Brand Brief (Amazon-first, DTC-bound)

Who We Are
- Trailblaze Outfitters builds field-tested outdoor gear for hikers and campers who value reliability without the luxury-markup.
- Our promise: “Gear that won’t quit when the trail gets rough.”
- Differentiators: rugged materials, obsessive QA (drop, soak, and stress tests), honest specs (no inflated lumen or capacity claims), lifetime support mindset, and eco-conscious packaging.

Core Audience
- Thru-hikers and backpackers who want sub-4 lb shelter systems and carbon trekking poles.
- Car campers and weekenders seeking durable, simple-to-use cooking and lighting kits.
- Budget-conscious buyers graduating from big-box entry-level gear to trustworthy mid-tier performance.

Brand Tone and Content Style
- Voice: pragmatic, trail-smart, and helpful. Always explain the “why” behind features.
- Visuals: natural light, realistic environments (mud, wet ground, pine needles), and real people using gear. Avoid overly glossy studio hero shots for DTC—favor a mix of lifestyle + clean product angles.
- UGC priority: campsite setup time-lapses, headlamp beam shots, and pole impact tests.

Key SKUs (for reference in content and merchandising)
- TB-TentPro-2P — 2-person backpacking tent (3.0 lb trail weight)
- TB-CookKit-4 — 4-piece camp cook set (hard-anodized aluminum)
- TB-HeadlampX — 400-lumen rechargeable headlamp
- TB-UltraTrek-Poles — Carbon trekking poles (pair)

Positioning & Differentiators by SKU
- TB-TentPro-2P: 3-minute pitch setup; reinforced guy-out points; genuine 2000 mm HH fly; tested in 35 mph wind tunnel.
- TB-CookKit-4: Nesting design with locking lid, even heat dispersion; anodized finish resists sticking and burn-on.
- TB-HeadlampX: True 400 lumen peak verified with lab-integrating sphere; USB-C recharge; lockout mode for pack carriage.
- TB-UltraTrek-Poles: 3K carbon with tungsten tips; easy lever-lock; foam extended grips for side-hilling.

Amazon to Shopify Context
- Current status: Amazon-first with growing repeat buyers. Reviews highlight reliability and honest specs.
- Customer feedback themes: quick setup (Tents), compactness (CookKit), real lumen output (HeadlampX), and weight-to-strength (Poles).
- Goal: Launch Shopify to build owned audience, lower blended fees, and create richer product education (comparison guides, setup videos), while keeping Amazon healthy.

Compliance & Email Capture (Amazon TOS-Compliant Only)
- Insert cards: Informational only. No review incentives. Direct customers to “Register your gear for extended 2-year coverage + trail map PDFs” at our domain. CTA: product registration/warranty portal. This is compliant because it does not incentivize reviews and focuses on product support/warranty.
- Product registration: Collect email and proof-of-purchase on our site. Provide compliant bonuses (setup guides, packing checklists, trail GPX bundle). Clearly state that registration is optional and benefits are support-related.
- Review requests: Use Amazon’s “Request a Review” button or Amazon-compliant neutral language. Do not direct review solicitation off-Amazon. No coupons or giveaways tied to reviews. Compliant reminder email flows can reference usage tips/troubleshooting and a neutral review ask via Amazon’s own system.
- Branding on inserts: Include Trailblaze Outfitters logo, brand story, support URL, and QR to product registration. Avoid any language that tries to divert the transaction or request external reviews.

DTC Store Edge (Why Shopify)
- Storytelling and education: setup demos, side-by-side spec comparisons, packability gifs, downloadable checklists.
- Bundles and upsells: “Fast Camp Starter” (TB-TentPro-2P + TB-HeadlampX), “Trail Kitchen” (TB-CookKit-4 + fuel guide).
- Community: email list for route reports, seasonal packing lists, and product-care micro-lessons.

MAP & Discount Philosophy
- MAP protected to preserve value and retailer relationships.
- Discount windows: 2-3 seasonal promos max (Memorial Day, Labor Day, Black Friday/Cyber Monday) and occasional value-add bundles rather than deep percentage cuts.
- Price integrity: Align Amazon and Shopify pricing within MAP rules; leverage bundles and gift-with-purchase for DTC differentiation.

Packaging & Unboxing (DTC)
- Recycled cardboard, minimal inks, QR to setup videos.
- Include: welcome card, care tips, warranty registration QR, safety disclaimer for burners and battery devices.

Initial DTC Content Priorities
- Hero landing pages: TB-TentPro-2P and TB-HeadlampX with setup/usage videos.
- Comparison guides: “2P Tent Buying Guide Under 4 lb” and “How Bright is 400 Lumens Outdoors?”
- Evergreen blog topics: shoulder-season camping, cookset care, headlamp battery maintenance, carbon pole usage tips.

Customer Support Principles
- 24-hour weekday response times; pre-written troubleshooting trees per SKU.
- Clear warranty copy (registration extends coverage from 1 to 2 years).
- NPS feedback collected post-purchase (Shopify) for iterative improvements.

Notes for Strategy Tailoring
- Targeted first DTC wins: capitalize on proven Amazon traction for TB-TentPro-2P and TB-HeadlampX with education-rich pages.
- Retention thesis: education + care content drives repeat for accessories and replacement parts, growing LTV.
