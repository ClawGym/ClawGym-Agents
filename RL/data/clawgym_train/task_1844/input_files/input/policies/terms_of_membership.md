Riverside Film Society – Membership Terms
Effective date: 2023-09-15

Tickets and refunds
Tickets are transferable. Refunds are available if you request cancellation at least 24 hours before the screening. We may deduct processing fees.

Code of Conduct
We are committed to a welcoming environment. Harassment, abusive behavior, and hate speech are not tolerated. The committee may remove attendees who violate this code of conduct.

Photography and filming
Our events may be photographed or filmed to document community activities. By attending, you consent to reasonable photography and filming. If you have concerns, speak to a committee member on arrival.

Safety and accessibility
We will communicate any content notes where available and make reasonable adjustments to ensure accessibility.
