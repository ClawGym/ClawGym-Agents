Section 1 — Declarations
- Coverage A (Dwelling) Limit: $450,000 (Replacement Cost)
- Coverage B (Other Structures) Limit: 10% of Coverage A ($45,000)
- Coverage C (Personal Property) Limit: 50% of Coverage A ($225,000)
- Coverage D (Loss of Use) Limit: 20% of Coverage A ($90,000), up to 12 months
- All-Peril Deductible: $1,500 per occurrence
- Wind/Hail Deductible: 1% of Coverage A per occurrence ($4,500)
- Included Endorsements: Sewer and Drain Backup Endorsement; Ordinance or Law 10%; Inflation Guard 4%
- Not Included: Roof Replacement Cost Endorsement HO-123; Scheduled Personal Property—Jewelry; Animal Liability Coverage HO-24X; Earthquake Coverage HO-170

Section 2 — Coverages
Coverage A — Dwelling
- We cover the dwelling on the “residence premises” shown in the Declarations at Replacement Cost, subject to policy terms.
- Limit: $450,000.
- Deductible: $1,500 per occurrence; Wind/Hail deductible applies when the loss is caused by windstorm or hail.

Coverage B — Other Structures
- We cover structures on the residence premises set apart from the dwelling.
- Limit: 10% of Coverage A ($45,000).
- Deductible: $1,500 per occurrence.

Coverage C — Personal Property
- Limit: $225,000 (50% of Coverage A).
- Deductible: $1,500 per occurrence.
- Special limits of liability apply:
  • Jewelry, watches, and furs: $2,500 for theft per occurrence.
  • Firearms: $2,000 per occurrence.
  • Silverware, goldware, and pewterware: $3,000 per occurrence.
  • Electronics (non-business): $5,000 per item.

Coverage D — Loss of Use
- Limit: $90,000 (20% of Coverage A), up to 12 months.
- We pay Additional Living Expense and Fair Rental Value for the shortest reasonable time required to repair or replace the premises or to permanently relocate.
- Loss of Use applies only when a covered loss causes the residence premises to become uninhabitable.

Additional Coverages
- Debris Removal: up to $5,000 per occurrence.
- Ordinance or Law: up to 10% of Coverage A.
- Identity Fraud Expense: up to $10,000 per policy period.
- Mold/Fungus/Wet Rot: remediation limited to $5,000 per policy period when resulting from a covered loss.

Section 3 — Exclusions
- We do not cover flood, surface water, tidal water, overflow of any body of water, or spray from any of these, whether driven by wind or not.
- We do not cover earth movement, including earthquake, landslide, or subsidence, regardless of cause.
- We do not cover wear and tear, marring, deterioration, latent defect, mechanical breakdown, or smog.
- We do not cover insects, rodents, birds, or vermin, including damage they cause.
- We do not cover intentional loss or any loss arising from illegal acts by an insured.
- We do not cover water or steam that seeps or leaks over time from plumbing or appliances, unless the seepage is sudden and accidental and discovered within 14 days of inception.
- Business property is limited to $2,500 on the residence premises and $500 off premises.
- Animal liability is excluded unless an Animal Liability endorsement is added.
- Earthquake is excluded unless HO-170 Earthquake Coverage is added.

Section 4 — Deductibles and Settlement
- All-Peril Deductible: $1,500 per occurrence applies unless a separate deductible applies.
- Wind/Hail Deductible: 1% of Coverage A ($4,500) per occurrence when the loss is caused by windstorm or hail.
- Roof surfacing Actual Cash Value (ACV) settlement applies when roof surfaces are older than 15 years at the time of loss unless Roof Replacement Cost Endorsement HO-123 is added. Roof installed: 2010.
- For covered personal property, settlement is at Replacement Cost for most items, subject to special limits and policy terms.

Section 5 — Endorsements
Sewer and Drain Backup Endorsement — Included
- We cover direct loss caused by water or water-borne material that backs up or overflows from a sewer or drain, including sump pump discharge or overflow.
- Limit: $10,000 per occurrence.
- Deductible: $1,000 per occurrence.

Ordinance or Law — Included
- We pay for increased costs due to the enforcement of any ordinance or law regulating the construction, repair, or demolition of the dwelling, up to 10% of Coverage A.

Inflation Guard — Included
- The Coverage A limit will increase during the policy period by 4% on a pro rata basis.

Endorsements available but not part of this policy:
- Roof Replacement Cost Endorsement HO-123 — Not included.
- Scheduled Personal Property—Jewelry — Not included.
- Animal Liability Coverage HO-24X — Not included.
- Earthquake Coverage HO-170 — Not included.

Section 6 — Definitions
- Deductible means the amount you pay out of pocket per covered loss before we pay.
- Premium means the price you pay for insurance for the policy term.
- Replacement Cost means the cost to replace with like kind and quality without deduction for depreciation.
- Actual Cash Value (ACV) means Replacement Cost at the time of loss minus depreciation.
- Endorsement means a written change to this policy.
- Occurrence means an accident, including continuous or repeated exposure to substantially the same general harmful conditions.
- Water backup means water or water-borne material that backs up or overflows from a sewer or drain.

Section 7 — Conditions
- Duties After Loss: Give prompt notice, protect the property, show the damaged property, and provide records and receipts when requested.
- Appraisal: If we and you fail to agree on the amount of loss, either may demand an appraisal.
- This policy does not provide coverage for flood. Flood coverage, if any, is provided only by a separate flood rider or policy.
