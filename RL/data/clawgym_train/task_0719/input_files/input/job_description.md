Stratus Commerce — Senior Backend Engineer
Location: Remote (US)
About Stratus Commerce
Stratus Commerce powers modern e-commerce experiences for mid-market retailers with a modular platform for catalog, orders, and fulfillment. We are scaling our backend platform to support rapid growth and are seeking a Senior Backend Engineer to drive reliability, performance, and developer experience.

What you’ll do
- Design, build, and operate backend services that power catalog, ordering, and fulfillment flows.
- Own service lifecycle: design reviews, implementation, testing, deployment, and production operations.
- Partner with product, design, and data to deliver high-quality features with measurable impact.
- Uphold engineering standards through code reviews, mentorship, and documentation.

Requirements (Must-haves)
- 5+ years professional experience building backend services in Python (FastAPI/Flask) or Go.
- Proven experience designing and scaling RESTful APIs and working with GraphQL.
- Strong with AWS services (ECS or Lambda) and messaging (SQS/SNS).
- Proficient with relational databases (Postgres) and caching (Redis).
- Experience with microservices and event-driven architectures.
- CI/CD ownership (GitHub Actions, CircleCI) and strong testing practices.
- Collaborative communication skills and product mindset.

Nice to have
- Kubernetes and Terraform experience.
- Kafka or similar streaming platforms for asynchronous workflows.
- Observability expertise (Datadog, OpenTelemetry).
- Security and compliance familiarity (SOC 2, ISO 27001).
- E-commerce domain experience and understanding of order lifecycles.
- Experience mentoring other engineers.

Compensation
- Competitive salary, equity, and benefits. Salary range: $160k–$190k base, DOE.

How to apply
- Apply at https://stratuscommerce.com/careers/senior-backend-engineer with your resume and a brief note about your most impactful backend project.
