City of Riverside Municipal Code (excerpts)

Section 8.12 — Alcohol in Public Parks:
Alcoholic beverages are prohibited in city parks unless an Alcohol Service Endorsement is issued as part of the Park Event Permit. If 'Alcohol Allowed: No' appears on the permit, possession or consumption is prohibited.

Section 9.45 — Tobacco and Vapor Products at Events:
Distribution, sale, or giveaway of tobacco or vapor products is prohibited at city-permitted events.

Section 10.05 — Animals at City Events:
Only service animals are permitted at city events unless the permit explicitly authorizes other animals.
