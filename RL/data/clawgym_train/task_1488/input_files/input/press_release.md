HelioGen Labs Announces Readiness for Riverbend Gene Drive Pilot

HelioGen Labs today announced that its NovaDrive mosquito strain has met internal safety benchmarks for a limited field trial. The pilot will be conducted in coordination with local stakeholders and subject to regulatory review.

Participants attending company-led demonstrations may be asked to sign a liability waiver acknowledging the experimental nature of the pilot. While the company is confident in its safety protocols, HelioGen Labs assumes no liability for incidental ecological shifts outside the defined monitoring zone.

The company has prepared a contingency plan and containment procedures designed to minimize risk in the unlikely event of deviation from expected outcomes. Community outreach sessions will continue throughout the trial period to discuss ethics, safety, and monitoring.

Regulatory filings remain on track, and the firm underscores that off-target effects were not observed in bench tests under simulated conditions.
