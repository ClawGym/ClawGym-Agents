# Chapter 3 — Company and Industry Background

## 3.1 Company Overview
NovaMart China sells imported beauty, wellness supplements, and snack products through Tmall Global, JD Worldwide, and Douyin. The business relies on bonded warehouse fulfillment in Hangzhou and Shenzhen. It utilizes platform ads, coupons, and KOL live-stream slots.

Key metrics:
- GMV: RMB 1.2B (2022), 1.55B (2023), 1.78B (2024)
- Contribution margin: 18% (2022), 14% (2023), 12% (2024)
- Douyin share of GMV: 28% → 37% → 43%
- Average fulfillment cost per order (2024): RMB 12.3 (incl. last-mile subsidies)

## 3.2 Category Characteristics
Beauty and wellness categories are promotion-sensitive and influenced by KOL recommendations. Shelf-life concerns and import compliance affect assortment. Demand is spiky around shopping festivals and live-stream events, creating forecasting challenges.

## 3.3 Competitive Landscape
Local brands (e.g., “FastJoy” and “Yanyan Beauty”) move quickly with trend-based product launches and aggressive couponing. Private labels use lower costs and faster content iteration. Competition intensifies during festival campaigns and platform traffic “wars.”

## 3.4 Platform Rules and Policy Context
Advertising content is subject to review. Creative approvals for cross-border content often require stricter checks for claims and translations. Platform algorithms reward watch time and click-through rates (CTR). Consumer protection policies increase return liabilities for misleading promotions.

Indicative operational reality:
- Creative approval SLA: ~72 hours for NovaMart (global review processes)
- Local competitors report ~12–18 hours
- Missed trend windows can reduce CTR by ~0.8–1.2 percentage points

## 3.5 Operations Snapshot
Live-stream campaigns drive sharp volume spikes but also stockouts and cancellations if inventory buffers are thin. Rebate expense ratio rose from 14% (2022) to 19% (2024). Return rates increase when deep coupons are combined with broad audience targeting; next-day order cancellations can spike to ~14% (vs. 7% baseline).

## 3.6 Summary
NovaMart China scaled GMV but now faces profitability pressure due to promotion intensity, variable fulfillment costs, and operational frictions around content, approvals, and inventory during campaign peaks.
