# Health Crises in Lublin Voivodeship (1918-1920) — Exhibit Draft

## Background
This draft panels a concise view of local mortality during the influenza pandemic and concurrent outbreaks of cholera and typhus in Lublin Voivodeship. Figures below are provisional and need verification against compiled local registers.

## Findings
- Influenza deaths in Lublin county during 1918-1920 exceeded 300, likely around 350.
- Cholera virtually disappeared by 1920 in Lublin county: only 0-1 deaths total in 1919-1920.
- Typhus mortality across Lublin Voivodeship in 1918-1920 was under 120.
- In 1918, Zamosc likely lost fewer people to influenza than Chelm did.
