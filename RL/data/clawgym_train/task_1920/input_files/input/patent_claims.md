Patent Number: US7654321
Title: Wireless Door Entry Sensor with Tamper Detection and Status Reporting

Independent Claim 1:

A door entry monitoring device comprising:
(a) a housing adapted to be mounted to a door frame;
(b) a magnetic sensing element positioned within the housing and configured to detect proximity of a magnet affixed to a door, the magnetic sensing element generating a first signal indicative of an open state and a second signal indicative of a closed state;
(c) a microcontroller operatively coupled to the magnetic sensing element and configured to detect transitions between the open and closed states responsive to the first and second signals;
(d) a wireless transceiver coupled to the microcontroller and configured to transmit, over a short-range wireless network, a state-change message upon detection of a transition, wherein the state-change message includes a unique device identifier associated with the device;
(e) a battery power source disposed within the housing and supplying power to the microcontroller and the wireless transceiver;
(f) a tamper-detection switch disposed to detect removal of the housing from the door frame, the tamper-detection switch providing a tamper signal to the microcontroller; and
(g) memory storing pairing information that is used by the microcontroller and the wireless transceiver to authenticate communications with a gateway;
wherein the microcontroller is further configured to include battery status information in the state-change message.
