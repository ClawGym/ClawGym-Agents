# Strategic Forks for Korean Equities Portfolio

## Fork A: Rotate into current leadership (cyclicals/consumer tech)
- Action: Increase exposure to Automotive and Consumer Electronics while trimming Semiconductors and Batteries near-term.
- Rationale: Sector rankings show leadership in Automotive and Consumer Electronics; Internet/Platform is positive and stable.
- Implementation:
  - Add on strength: Hyundai Motor, LG Electronics.
  - Maintain core but lighter weights in Samsung Electronics; hold NAVER baseline.
  - Tight risk on semis/batteries until momentum improves.
- Risk management:
  - Use stop-loss bands of ~5% from entry on new adds.
  - If Semiconductors remain bottom-3 for 3 consecutive sessions, keep underweight.

## Fork B: Buy-the-dip in laggards (semis/batteries)
- Action: Overweight SK hynix and LG Energy Solution on weakness, expecting recovery into H2 on HBM/EV demand.
- Rationale: Disclosures flag capacity expansion (HBM) and new EV contracts; drawdowns may present favorable entries.
- Implementation:
  - Stage entries in 2–3 tranches over one week.
  - Keep NAVER and Celltrion as balance for growth/defensives.
- Risk management:
  - If SK hynix downtime exceeds 72 hours in subsequent disclosures, defer adds.
  - If LGES provisions escalate above KRW 200b, delay second tranche.
  - Abandon plan if Batteries and Semiconductors both stay bottom-3 for the week.

## Cross-cutting concerns
- Earnings quality vs. one-offs (e.g., warranty provisions).
- Event risk from operational incidents.
- Sector breadth and persistence (leadership vs. laggards).
- Maintain cash buffer of ~10% to manage volatility.
