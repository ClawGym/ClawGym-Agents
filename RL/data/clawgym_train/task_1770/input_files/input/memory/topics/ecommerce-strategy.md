# E-Commerce Strategy — Acme Clean

## Channels
- Amazon: Focus on high-visibility keywords (eco-friendly cleaner, plant-based, non-toxic).
- Shopify: Build education-centric landing pages that explain benefits and safety credentials.

## Positioning
Acme Clean positions itself as the practical, planet-friendly household cleaner designed for busy urban households who want performance without harsh chemicals.

## Differentiators
- Plant-based surfactants with independently verified lab results on stain removal.
- Recyclable packaging and refill pouches to reduce plastic.
- Transparent ingredient list and clear usage instructions.

## Voice & Tone
- Helpful, pragmatic, friendly.
- Avoid greenwashing claims; cite specific facts (e.g., “readily biodegradable”).
- Use concise bullets for product pages and short benefit headlines.

## Visual Identity Cues
- Clean, airy layouts with generous white space.
- Color accents: fresh green (#2E7D32), ocean blue (#0277BD), warm neutral (#F5F5F5).
- Photography: real-life kitchen/bath settings, natural light, minimal props.

## KPIs
- Conversion rate > 6% on Amazon detail pages for top listings.
- Return customer rate > 20% on Shopify within 90 days.
- Review sentiment: > 4.5 average with emphasis on “smell” and “no residue.”
