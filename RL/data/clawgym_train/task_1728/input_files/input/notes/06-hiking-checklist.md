# Hiking Checklist

Checklist Items:
- Water (at least 2 liters per person)
- Snacks and lunch
- Map and compass
- First aid kit
- Headlamp with spare batteries
- Extra layers and rain shell
- Hat and sunscreen
- Emergency blanket
- Multi-tool
- Trekking poles (optional)

Pre-trip:
- Check weather and trail conditions.
- Tell someone your route and return time.
- Charge phone and pack a small battery.

On-trail:
- Pace evenly, take small breaks.
- Leave no trace principles.
