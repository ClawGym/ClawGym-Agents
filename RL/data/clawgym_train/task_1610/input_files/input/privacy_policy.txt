WellLife Privacy Policy (Effective 2026-02-15)

Scope
This policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use the WellLife program.

Information We Collect
• Wearable and app data (e.g., steps, heart rate, sleep duration)
• Questionnaire responses about wellness habits
• Device identifiers and diagnostic logs
• Approximate location inferred from IP address for fraud prevention and service localization

How We Use Information
• To provide coaching and recommendations
• To maintain and improve the service
• To conduct analytics and program evaluation

Sharing
• We may share pseudonymous identifiers with third‑party analytics providers
• We may share de‑identified datasets with research partners

Retention
• We retain personal data for up to 36 months from last activity unless a longer period is required for legal compliance or dispute resolution

Your Choices
• You can request deletion of your personal data; upon confirmed request, we aim to delete within 30 days

Security
• We use encryption and access controls to protect your information

Changes to This Policy
• We will post updates with a new effective date when changes occur
