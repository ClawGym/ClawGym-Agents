# FlowForge — Product Marketing Context

Last updated: April 2026

## Positioning Statement
FlowForge is the workflow automation platform built for operations teams in mid‑market manufacturing and logistics. We connect your ERP, WMS, MES, and spreadsheets to remove manual touches, automate exceptions, and keep work moving—without ripping and replacing your systems.

- Category: Operations workflow automation (low-code)
- Target ICP: Mid-market manufacturers and logistics providers (200–2,000 employees) in North America
- Primary Buyers: Operations Directors/VPs; Champions: Operations Managers, Industrial Engineers

## Core Value Proposition
Automate the work between systems and teams:
- Fewer manual touches per order and per shipment
- Faster exception resolution and approvals
- Clear audit trails for compliance and customers
- Real-time visibility to SLAs, bottlenecks, and handoffs

Typical outcomes within 60–90 days:
- 20–40% fewer manual touches on target process
- 10–25% faster cycle time (order-to-cash, dock-to-stock, NCRs)
- +3–7 points improvement in OTD/OTIF
- 50–75% fewer status-chasing emails and spreadsheets

## Differentiators
1. Manufacturing + Logistics Native
   - Prebuilt connectors and data models for ERP (SAP Business One, NetSuite, Microsoft Dynamics), WMS (Manhattan, Blue Yonder), MES (Ignition, Tulip), TMS, and EDI (SPS Commerce).
2. Human-in-the-Loop by Design
   - Approvals, escalations, and ad-hoc tasks embedded inside automated flows (mobile and desktop).
3. Fast Time to Value
   - Library of 40+ proven templates (e.g., ASN exception handler, supplier onboarding, ECN approvals). First workflow live in 30 days.
4. Governance & Compliance
   - Role-based access, field-level encryption, full audit trails, SOC 2 Type II, SSO (SAML), data residency options.
5. No Rip-and-Replace
   - Works over your stack; hybrid connectors for on-prem and cloud.

## Key Use Cases
- Order-to-cash handoffs (order validation, credit holds, ship date commits)
- Warehouse exceptions (ASN mismatch, short pick, dock scheduling)
- Non-conformance/CAPA workflows (quality, supplier NCRs)
- Supplier onboarding and compliance document collection
- Engineering change notices (ECN/ECO) approvals
- Inventory cycle count exceptions and recount coordination
- Transportation exceptions (missed scans, reweigh/reclass, carrier tendering)
- EDI error handling and reprocessing

## Product Capabilities
- Low-code flow builder with versioning and reusable subflows
- 120+ native connectors + secure SQL/HTTP connectors
- SLA timers, business calendars, and working hours logic
- Task inbox for approvals and manual steps (web + mobile)
- Ops dashboards and bottleneck analytics
- Error handling, retries, alerting, and fallback tasks
- Environments: Cloud with private agent for on‑prem systems

## Objections & Responses
- “We already have an ERP/WMS workflow module.”
  - Most modules don’t span cross‑system exceptions or human tasks across teams. FlowForge sits across systems to close gaps and escalate with context.
- “IT bandwidth is limited.”
  - Low-code with prebuilt templates. We implement the first two workflows with you in 30 days. No rip-and-replace, minimal IT lift.
- “Security/compliance?”
  - SOC 2 Type II, SSO (SAML), audit trails, field-level encryption, access controls, and private agent for on‑prem integration.
- “Will this last if we upgrade systems?”
  - Flows are abstracted from underlying systems; swapping connectors preserves business logic.

## Competitive Landscape (High-Level)
- iPaaS (generic): Broad connectors, weak human tasks and ops templates.
- RPA: Great for UI tasks; not ideal for cross‑team workflows with approvals.
- In‑app workflow modules: Siloed to one system; limited cross‑system orchestration.

## Brand Guardrails
- Value before ask: lead with checklists, benchmarks, and templates.
- Specific, ops‑oriented language: manual touches, cycle time, exceptions, SLAs.
- Avoid hype; be pragmatic and metric‑driven.
