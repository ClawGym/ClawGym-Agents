An AI-powered math tutoring platform for U.S. middle and high school students that adapts explanations to each learner’s level, provides step-by-step solutions in clear LaTeX, detects common mistakes in real time (sign errors, formula misapplication), and offers teachers scaffolded problem sets with analytics. The app integrates with major LMS systems, supports offline practice on mobile, and emphasizes encouragement and short, visual explanations for younger learners.
