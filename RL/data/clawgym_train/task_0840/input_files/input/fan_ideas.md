# Fan Engagement & Revenue Ideas (Draft)

As a long-time season ticket holder, I'd love to pilot a couple of ideas to boost in-arena experience and per-cap revenue.

Ideas:
- Family Pack + Concession Voucher: For families purchasing the Family Pack, include a $15 concession voucher add-on to encourage in-seat spending.
- Theme Night Merch Discount: Offer a 10% in-arena merchandise discount to anyone with a Theme Night bundle to increase attachment rates.
- Mobile Pre-Order Perk: Give early pickup windows for mobile food pre-orders to reduce lines and raise throughput.

- Past engagement metrics by promotion type (CSV)
- Current membership/ticket packages and add-ons (HTML)
