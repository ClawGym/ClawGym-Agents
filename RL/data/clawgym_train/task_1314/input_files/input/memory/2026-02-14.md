## lesson
### Avoid averaging down options losers
Lesson learned from options trading: never add to a losing position. Averaging down on calls increased total debit and deepened the drawdown when IV contracted. Example: first contract +$1,100 unrealized, added two lower, total ended -$3,200 after IV crush. Rule: set max loss and close—don’t escalate. Keywords: options, trading, mistake, discipline.

## decision
### Switching to call debit spreads in elevated IV
Decision: when IV > HV by >20pp, switch from naked calls to call debit spreads. Rationale: options are expensive; spreads cap premium paid and reduce theta burn. Use ATM/near-ATM structure, 2–4 weeks out, on names we actually follow. Applies to trading plays pre-earnings or catalysts. Keywords: decision, options, IV, trading, discipline.
