# Notes and Quotations on Brunelleschi's Works

## Santa Maria del Fiore (Dome)
> The octagonal double-shell solution balanced mass and thrust, allowing the span without centering.

## Pazzi Chapel
> Its serene geometry masks a sophisticated load path, with the dome settling cleanly onto the pendentives.

## Basilica of San Lorenzo
> The modular bay sets a didactic cadence; Brunelleschi’s proportions coordinate aisle and nave with lucid clarity.

## Ospedale degli Innocenti
> The arcade announces a new civic scale; the measured bay repeats a humane rhythm along the square.
