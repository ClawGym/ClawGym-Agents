Jordan Ellis: Beyond the Box Score

Jordan Ellis retired after a remarkable career that blended excellence on the court with sustained community engagement.

On-Court Achievements

  • 3x League MVP (2011, 2013, 2015)
  • 2 Championships (2012, 2015)
  • Career points: 21,345
  • Assists: 6,432
  • Games played: 1,234

Off-Court Contributions

  • Founded the Ellis Scholars Fund in 2010; has awarded 120 scholarships to date.
  • Donated over $3.2 million in community grants since 2012.
  • Launched the "Hoops & Homework" initiative, reaching 2,400 students in 2020.
  • Opened 4 Youth Learning Centers between 2014 and 2018.

Ellis has become a model for how athletes can leverage their platform for societal good.
