Title: Midnight Reflections
Year: 1984
---
I remember the first night we shot the rain-soaked finale on the backlot. The crew was soaked but cheerful, and the scene felt electric. The director laughed as the wind machines went wild, and my co-star improvised a gentle, surprising line that softened the moment. I felt proud, nervous, and grateful. We wrapped late, exhausted but thrilled, and I still smile when I hear that music swell.
