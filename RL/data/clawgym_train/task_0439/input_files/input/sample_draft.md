# A Bottle Episode in Black & White

Cold open: the kitchen erupts in I Love Lucy chaos—pots clatter, plans backfire, and heart wins the scene.

Act One: a Twilight Zone morality play twist creeps in just before the act break, not flashy—just unsettling, like a whisper from Serling.

Act Two: the ensemble breathes with the warmth of M*A*S*H, the 4077th spirit of gallows humor giving way to grace. Hawkeye-like quips soften the edges.

Final beat: a button that winks at The Mary Tyler Moore Show, newsroom energy and all, but with a modern cadence.

Tone note: nostalgia is seasoning, not the stew. The homage should feel lived-in rather than name-dropped.
