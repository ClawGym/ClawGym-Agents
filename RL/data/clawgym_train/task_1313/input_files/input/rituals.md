# Our Lucky Ways

- Wear the blue socks on game-day (always left sock first!!)
- clap 7 times after the anthem? 
- Bring the community scarf, wave it clockwise 3 turns, but ONLY if it's a home match
- eat oranges at halftime, share slices for team unity (if available) 
- Chant: "One team, one heart, all in!" three breaths: in, out, in.
- DO NOT walk under ladders near the stadium entrances (bad vibes)
- Coin toss ritual: shiny coin in right shoe until kickoff; remove at seat
- bring bell to ring at 17' and 71' ... unless security says no -> then, keychain jingle
- Water the lucky fern by the TV pregame (for watch parties).
