# Custom Journals from Vintage Books
I offer commissions where I refit worn but beautiful covers with fresh, archival paper for daily use. Each notebook is tailored—page style, closure, and size—so it becomes a practical object with a bit of history. If you have a family book or a thrifted cover you love, please [get in touch](/contact) to discuss feasibility.

Turnaround depends on the condition of the original and the binding method we choose. I keep notes on light conservation, simple closures, and long-stitch bindings so the final object remains sturdy without looking overdone.
