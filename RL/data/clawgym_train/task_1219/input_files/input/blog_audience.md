# Audience & Editorial Focus (Milan Insider)

- Readers: 25–40, Milan-based creative professionals; value behind-the-scenes access, ateliers, and emerging designers.
- Strong interest drivers: sustainability, craftsmanship, material innovation, limited or intimate formats.
- Lower interest: mass-market sales events or discount-driven pop-ups unless there is a unique artisan angle.
- Scheduling preferences: weekday evenings (especially Thu–Fri, 18:00–22:00). Weekends are acceptable but Sundays are generally avoided. Conflicts reduce coverage likelihood.
- Location: central neighborhoods (Brera, Garibaldi/Isola, Porta Romana) are ideal; far-flung locations reduce likelihood.
- Photography: access is important; total bans are disfavored, limited windows are acceptable if access is meaningful for coverage.
- Timeliness: if RSVP deadline has passed, coverage likelihood drops unless the fit is exceptional.
