Community Health and Safety Tips for Lae Urban

Prepared by local health volunteers.

Rainy Season Preparedness

  • Boil or treat drinking water when heavy rains occur.
  • Clear drains and gutters around your home to reduce flooding.
  • Store food in sealed containers and wash hands with soap regularly.
  • Keep a small emergency kit (torch, batteries, first aid) ready at home.

Road Safety Reminders

  1. Slow down on wet roads and watch for pedestrians.
  2. Wear helmets when riding motorbikes and bicycles.
  3. Report potholes and hazards to your ward councillor.
