- Case: NovaCore (remote-first SaaS, ~420 employees across US/EU/APAC). Before FlowGrid:
  - Avg. meeting load: 27 hours/week per knowledge worker; only ~3.2 hours/week of 2h+ “focus time”.
  - Recurring meetings lacked agenda discipline; decisions scattered in chats.
- Intervention using FlowGrid over 8 weeks:
  1) Agenda discipline via templated agendas and “meeting hygiene” check (timeboxing, purpose, owner).
  2) Shift to async collaboration for updates: documentation-first summaries + Loom video updates; live time reserved for decisions or blockers.
  3) Decision logs standardized with RACI on owners and due dates; searchable in FlowGrid.
  4) Focus Guard: protected “focus time” blocks and automatic guardrails against calendar debt.
- Outcomes at NovaCore by week 8:
  - 38% reduction in recurring meeting hours (from 27h to ~16.7h/week).
  - +6.1 hours/week regained focus time (2h+ blocks).
  - +61% more decisions captured in decision logs; cycle time to decision down 14%.
  - Meeting Health score up 22 points; pulse survey open-text mentions fewer “performative” status calls.
- Messaging nuance: Emphasize balance—reduce meetings without losing alignment. Present FlowGrid as an enabler (not a hard sell).
