## Policy Context
Regulatory frameworks can influence entry costs, licensing timelines, and compliance burdens. Evidence-informed adjustments can improve dynamism without compromising safety or consumer protection.

## Preliminary Options
- Streamline licensing processes with clear time limits and online submissions.
- Introduce graduated compliance requirements proportional to firm size.
- Pilot regulatory sandboxes for innovative business models.
