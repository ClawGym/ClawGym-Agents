# Personal Health

Guidelines and records to sustain energy and focus during intense project periods.

## Baseline
- Typical sleep: 7–7.5 hours
- Resting heart rate: 58–62 bpm
- Weekly runs: 3–4 sessions (3–6 km each)
- Strength: 2 sessions focusing on compound lifts
- Hydration: target 2.5 L/day

## Goals
- Maintain consistent sleep ≥ 7 hours during demo sprint
- Complete a 5K run sub-30 minutes without overexertion
- Keep caffeine under 300 mg/day
- Stretch 10 minutes after runs to reduce stiffness

### Running
- Warmup: 5 minutes easy jog + dynamic drills
- Pace strategy: negative split when possible
- Surfaces: prefer track or flat trail to spare joints
- Shoes: rotate pairs to reduce injury risk

## Nutrition
- Breakfast: protein-focused with complex carbs
- Lunch: balanced plate; avoid heavy fried foods on workdays
- Dinner: stop eating 2 hours before bed
- Snacks: fruit, yogurt, nuts

### Recovery
- Easy day after hard effort
- Mobility: hips, calves, hamstrings priority
- Watch HRV trend; avoid pushing when trending down

## Metrics
- Track sleep, HR, and run times weekly
- Note any pain > 2 days and deload if needed

### Notes
Keep entries short and factual. Weekly REM consolidation appends new facts to the end of this file.
