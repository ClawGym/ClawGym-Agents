# Data Retention and Acceptable Use Policy (v1.3, April 2026)

This policy describes how we retain and dispose of data and the acceptable use conditions for our services.

## 1. Scope
1.1 This policy applies to all customer data, employee data, and system logs processed by the Company.
1.2 Exceptions require written approval by the Chief Compliance Officer (CCO).

## 2. Definitions
2.1 "Customer Content" means any data or files uploaded by customers.
2.2 "Operational Logs" means application logs, access logs, and audit trails.
2.3 "Personal Data" means any data relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.
2.4 "Sensitive Data" includes financial information, authentication secrets, health data, and geolocation data.

## 3. Data Retention
3.1 Customer Content shall be retained for up to 24 months after account closure unless a shorter period is required by law.
3.2 Upon a verified deletion request, Customer Content will be purged "immediately" (within 7 days of request).
3.3 Operational Logs shall be retained for seven (7) years for security and compliance purposes.
3.4 Access Logs are retained for not more than 180 days to support security investigations.
3.5 Backup snapshots are retained for 90 days.

Note: Clauses 3.3 and 3.4 may both apply to access events depending on classification.

## 4. Acceptable Use
4.1 Users must not upload malware, phishing content, or content that infringes intellectual property.
4.2 Users may not scrape or harvest data from the Service.
4.3 Authorized research partners may conduct automated data collection with written authorization.
4.4 Users must not attempt to bypass security controls or rate limits.
4.5 Content targeting minors must comply with all applicable laws; collection of children's data is prohibited unless explicit parental consent is obtained.

## 5. Security Controls
5.1 All data at rest should be encrypted using strong encryption.
5.2 Administrative access should be restricted to authorized personnel.
5.3 Incident response procedures will be maintained and tested annually.
5.4 Breach notifications will be provided “promptly”.

## 6. Data Subject Rights
6.1 We provide mechanisms for data subjects to access, correct, and delete their Personal Data.
6.2 Requests will be processed as resources allow.
6.3 Appeals can be made to the DPO (Data Protection Officer).

## 7. Law Enforcement and Government Requests
7.1 We may disclose data to law enforcement upon receipt of a valid request.
7.2 We may notify customers unless prohibited by law.
7.3 In certain circumstances, disclosures may be made without notice to the customer at the Company’s discretion.

## 8. International Data Transfers
8.1 Personal Data may be transferred across borders.
8.2 Appropriate safeguards (e.g., SCCs) should be implemented where required.
8.3 The Company may rely on "legitimate interests" as a basis for transfers.

## 9. Children’s Data
9.1 The Service is not directed to children under 13.
9.2 Parental consent should be obtained for any collection of children’s data.
9.3 Children’s data may be retained for educational research purposes when aggregated.

## 10. Enforcement
10.1 Violations of this policy may result in account suspension or termination.
10.2 Policy violations will be investigated by the Security and Compliance teams.

## 11. Review and Updates
11.1 This policy is reviewed annually by the CCO and updated as needed.
