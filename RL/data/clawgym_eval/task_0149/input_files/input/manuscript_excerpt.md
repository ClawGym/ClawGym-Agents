# The Jade Kettle — Manuscript Excerpt

## Chapter 1: The Arrival

Mei pressed her forehead to the window as the city rose like a promise from the water.
In her pocket, the old coin from Amah warmed with the heat of her palm.
She rehearsed her new name under her breath, syllables like stepping-stones.

## Chapter 2: The Letter

The letter arrived on a rain-bright morning, Jin's handwriting tilting like a gust.
Mei traced the characters that Amah taught her, each stroke a thread back home.
Between the lines, silence gathered like tea leaves at the bottom of a cup.

## Chapter 3: The Ceremony

Steam folded over the bowls as Amah lifted the kettle, each motion a small ceremony.
Mei watched the tea darken, patience steeped into the room.
Outside, the city beat its own rhythm, tradition and neon braided in the window's reflection.
