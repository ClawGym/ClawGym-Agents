Title: Lantern Parade Watercolor
Year: 2023
Medium: Watercolor on cold press
Notes: Study of translucent lanterns and glow on dusk streets.
Palette: Cadmium red light, quinacridone rose, ultramarine, and sap green.
Wash: Variegated sky transitioning into evening hues.
Edges: Soft blends with lifted highlights for paper texture.
Figures: Silhouettes suggested with minimal strokes.
Layout: Procession rhythm leading into the square.
Materials: Natural bristle, sable round, masking fluid tests.
