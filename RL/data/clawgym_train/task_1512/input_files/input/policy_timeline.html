Policy Milestones

  • Paris Agreement Global pact to limit warming well below 2°C.
  • IPCC Special Report on 1.5°C Outlined pathways to limit warming to 1.5°C and highlighted benefits of rapid action.
  • Updated Nationally Determined Contributions Many countries strengthened their NDCs, signaling increased ambition.
  • Loss and Damage Funding Arrangement Agreement in principle to mobilize support for countries facing climate-related losses.
