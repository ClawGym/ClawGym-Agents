We are incredibly excited about the upcoming rally and want to share a detailed update with everyone who cares about both recreation and our local environment. Our team is committed to doing the right thing and we have started a preliminary screening of the planned route to understand any potential interactions with sensitive habitats and protected areas. We value transparency and will continue to communicate frequently and openly with the public as we progress.

While we expect the event to proceed smoothly, we also recognize that responsible planning requires us to look closely at access points, buffer needs, nesting considerations, and seasonal constraints. We are coordinating with organizers and volunteers to identify practical mitigation measures that keep the experience enjoyable while protecting wildlife and landscape features. The goal is to confirm a route that is safe, respectful, and aligned with preservation laws, and to put clear actions in place for any issues that arise.

We appreciate everyone’s support and patience as we finalize the route. We will provide more information as soon as the screening concludes and the action plan is ready. Your feedback matters, and we invite questions and ideas that help us improve our stewardship efforts for the rally and future events.
