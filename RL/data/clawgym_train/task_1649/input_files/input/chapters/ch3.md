# Chapter 3: Decisions and Detours

### Scene 1: Family planning
In a counseling session, the family talks through the implications of germline changes that would be passed to future children. A recent karyotype is reviewed alongside carrier screening to clarify risks. The counselor emphasizes that germline interventions remain controversial and regulated, and the couple asks for plain-language summaries to take home.

### Scene 2: Biohacking
At a community makerspace, a small group tests mail-order CRISPR kits. They set up a quick PCR to check a simple edit and chat about off-target issues they read about online. One attendee mentions trying a viral vector for a different project, while another asks about mitochondrial DNA and why it complicates some assays. The mentor reminds them that even basic CRISPR experiments require care and controls.
