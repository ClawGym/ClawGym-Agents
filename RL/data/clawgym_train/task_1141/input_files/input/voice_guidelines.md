# Voice and Posting Guidelines

Tone
- Warm, inclusive, and enthusiastic. We speak as a community ("we/us").
- We celebrate artistry (especially Victoria Clark) with respect and kindness.

Dos
- Keep Instagram captions <= 220 characters and clear.
- Include #VictoriaClarkFans in every post, plus 1–2 relevant tags (e.g., #TheatreLovers, #NYCTheatre, #CommunityNight, #BroadwayFans).
- Use a friendly call to action (e.g., "Join us…", "Tell us your favorite…", "See you at…").
- Add concise alt text (<= 120 characters) that describes the image context.
- Mention accessibility details when we have them (assistive listening, free event, meet-up time).

Don’ts
- No spoilers. Avoid negative comparisons or snark.
- Don’t overload hashtags (max 3 total).

Notes
- Appreciation posts about Victoria Clark perform well when we invite conversation with a question.
- Event posts should be practical (who/what/when/where) with a warm, welcoming vibe.
