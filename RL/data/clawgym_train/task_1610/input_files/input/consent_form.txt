WellLife Participant Consent Form (Rev. 2026-03)

Purpose
You are invited to participate in the WellLife wellness program to support healthy lifestyle choices. Participation is entirely voluntary.

Data Collected
We collect wearable metrics (steps, resting heart rate, sleep duration) and responses to in‑app questionnaires about wellness habits.

Use of Data
Your personal data will be used to provide coaching, generate your personalized insights, and evaluate program effectiveness.

Sharing
We may share de‑identified and aggregated program results with academic partners for research on wellness interventions.

Retention
Personal data will be retained for 24 months from last activity, unless a shorter period is required by law.

Withdrawal & Deletion
You may withdraw at any time without penalty. Upon a confirmed withdrawal request, we will delete your personal data within 45 days.

Risks
There is a small risk of unintended disclosure if de‑identified data were re‑identified, though safeguards are in place.

Voluntariness
Your employment status and benefits will not be affected by your decision to participate or not.
