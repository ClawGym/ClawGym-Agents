Sri Lanka Elections-Related Restrictions — Working Excerpts for Internal Review (non-authoritative)

- [EA-1] Do not publish any dataset that can reasonably identify how a named individual voted.
- [EA-2] Unofficial voter roll data containing names with addresses must not be published; only aggregated counts by district are allowed.
- [EA-3] Exit poll or near-poll interviews must be anonymized before publication; per-respondent microdata may not be released during an election period.
