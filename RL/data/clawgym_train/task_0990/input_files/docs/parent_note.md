Parent Note: Social-Communication Check-in

Dear Parents/Guardians,

We use a short questionnaire to understand each child's social-communication experiences. This tool is not a diagnosis. This quick check helps us spot red flags that may indicate we need to look more closely. Most children fall within normal development patterns, and a wide range of strengths are common.

We value differences in how kids think, connect, and learn. Our goal is to celebrate diverse communication styles and make supports available when helpful.

Thank you for your partnership,
The Clinic Team
