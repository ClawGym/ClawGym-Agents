---
title: The White Stag of Caer Donnan
location: North Gate Wood
century: 16
tags:
  - omen
  - hunt
narrator: Lady Elsbeth Donnan
---
They say that on the eve of the autumn fair, a pale stag appeared between the beeches by the north gate.
My grandmother swore it bowed its antlered head to the old crest on the arch and vanished without a sound.
For years, hunters would lay down their bows at that spot, fearing to chase what might be a messenger from older times.
