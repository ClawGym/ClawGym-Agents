Metrovale Department of Transportation
Office of Performance and Analytics
Statistics Release — April 2025

Scope: Annual 2024 results and early 2025 indicators for Metrovale public transit (bus and rail).

Key Findings
1) Ridership Growth
- 2023 total ridership: 120.0 million trips
- 2024 total ridership: 132.0 million trips
- Year-over-year change (2024 vs 2023): +10.0%
- Q1 2024 ridership: 31.5 million trips
- Q1 2025 ridership (preliminary): 34.0 million trips (+8% vs Q1 2024)

2) Fare Policy
- Base adult fare increased from $2.50 to $2.75 effective July 1, 2024.
- Concession fares for low-income programs and seniors remained unchanged in 2024.
- No additional fare changes are scheduled for calendar year 2025 as of this release.

3) Fleet Emissions (Lifecycle Estimates)
- Diesel bus (2018 baseline) CO2e intensity: 1.20 kg CO2e per km.
- Battery-electric bus (2024, grid-adjusted) CO2e intensity: 0.78 kg CO2e per km.
  - Approximate reduction vs diesel baseline: 35%.
  - Notes: Estimate includes vehicle operation (using Metrovale’s 2024 grid mix ~45% renewable) and amortized battery manufacturing emissions; excludes end-of-life recycling credits.
- Tailpipe CO2 for battery-electric buses is zero; lifecycle figure reflects upstream electricity and manufacturing.

Methods and Data Sources
- Fare data from MV-DOT fare schedules and tariff bulletins (effective dates as stated).
- Ridership from automated fare collection and APC systems; audited totals published herein.
- Emissions intensities derived from MV-DOT fleet tests and energy procurement data, with standard lifecycle factors.
