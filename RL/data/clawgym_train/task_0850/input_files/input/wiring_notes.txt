Controller: Arduino Uno R3 (5V logic)

Power:
- Uno powered from laptop USB (5V via USB)
- SG90 servo power: red wire to Arduino 5V pin; brown (GND) to Arduino GND; orange (signal) to D9
- DC motor power: separate 6V 4xAA battery pack to motor +; return to battery − (NOT currently tied to Uno GND)
- HC-05 Bluetooth: VCC to 5V, GND to GND, TX to D0 (RX), RX to D1 (TX) via direct jumper (no divider)
- No dedicated decoupling capacitors added near the BME280 or on servo/motor supply rails (no 0.1uF ceramic caps at sensor)

Sensors:
- BME280 breakout (3.3V-only breakout):
  - VCC to 3.3V pin on Uno
  - GND to GND
  - SDA to A4
  - SCL to A5
  - No level shifter used between 5V Uno I2C lines and 3.3V BME280 lines
  - Using ~30 cm Dupont jumpers for I2C wiring
  - No local 0.1uF decoupling capacitor at the BME280 board

Actuators:
- SG90 servo:
  - Signal: D9 (PWM)
  - Power: from Arduino 5V pin (same as Uno)
  - Ground: Arduino GND
- DC motor (small 3–6V “TT” type):
  - Driven through a 2N2222 NPN transistor on D3 (base via 1k resistor)
  - Motor + to 6V battery pack +
  - Motor − to transistor collector; transistor emitter to battery −
  - Flyback diode across motor terminals (1N4007, band to +)
  - Battery − (motor ground) is NOT currently connected/common with Arduino GND

User Interface:
- Pushbutton to D7:
  - One leg of the button to D7
  - Other leg to +5V
  - No resistor to ground; pinMode set to INPUT (not INPUT_PULLUP)
  - Result: when button is open, D7 is floating
- LED indicator:
  - D13 to LED anode directly
  - LED cathode to GND
  - No current-limiting resistor in series

Serial/Comms:
- Using Serial Monitor for debugging at 115200 (habit), but sketch may use 9600
- HC-05 connected on hardware RX/TX pins 0/1 (same as USB serial)
- When HC-05 is connected, uploads sometimes fail; Serial Monitor sometimes can’t open or shows garbage

Cable/Wiring Notes:
- Long jumper leads (~30 cm) to sensors and some power runs
- No twisted pairs or shielding
- Breadboard power rails used; possibly intermittent contacts
