# Outline: "Ashes of the Jubilee" (working title)

Act I

S1: A young journalist arrives in the city days after the upheaval, tasked with "objectivity" while secretly carrying a family memory of the event.
S2: An official inquiry opens; the journalist is assigned to cover it under a biased editor.

Act II

S3: The protagonist chases a lead that points to an overlooked neighborhood whose accounts contradict official timelines.
S4: Montage of "both sides" coverage that balances perspectives and diffuses blame, leading to meandering mid-plot.

Act III

S5: The inquiry report is released; the journalist must decide whether to publish a damning sidebar that breaks with the paper’s line.
