# DocuFlux AI — Pricing & Commercial Terms (Summary)

## Plans
- Standard: $2,000/month platform fee + $0.015/document
- Professional: $5,000/month + $0.012/document, includes EU residency option
- Enterprise: Custom pricing, includes SSO, CMK support, strict no-retention LLM endpoints, and enhanced support

Minimum term: 12 months

## Renewal & Termination
- Auto-renewal: Subscriptions renew for successive 12-month terms unless either party provides written non-renewal notice at least 60 days prior to the end of the then-current term.
- Early termination: Not permitted for convenience. Termination for cause allowed with 30-day cure period.
- Effect of termination: Access continues through end of term. Post-termination data handling per DPA (deletion within 30 days; backups 60 days).

## SLA & Service Credits
- Uptime commitment: 99.5% monthly.
- Credits:
  - 99.0%–99.5%: 5% of monthly fee
  - 98.0%–98.99%: 7.5% of monthly fee
  - <98.0%: 10% of monthly fee
- Credits are the sole and exclusive remedy for SLA breaches. No termination right for chronic downtime.
- Exclusions: Scheduled maintenance and third-party cloud provider outages.

## Support
- Standard: Business hours support (P1 2-hour initial response).
- Professional/Enterprise: 24x7 P1 response; escalation to on-call engineering.

## Price Changes
- Annual price adjustment: Up to 7% with 30 days’ notice.
- Extraordinary changes: Pass-through of third-party cost increases with 30 days’ notice (cap at +10% within a 12-month period).

## Data Ownership & Export
- Customer owns Customer Content and outputs. DocuFlux retains all rights to software, models, and derivative improvements.
- Exports: Self-serve via API/UI. Bulk offboarding exports completed within 10 business days; professional services billed at $200/hour if custom work is required.

## AI Output Disclaimer
- AI-generated outputs may contain inaccuracies. Customers are responsible for validating critical results. See MSA for limitations of liability.
