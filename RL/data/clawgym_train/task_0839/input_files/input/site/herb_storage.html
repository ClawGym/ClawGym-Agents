Tender Herb Storage and Packaging

To keep delicate herbs bright and crisp, I focus on packaging choices that balance humidity and airflow. Perforated produce bags help prevent condensation while limiting wilting. For longer holding, gentle moisture management with cellulose-based wraps can maintain turgor without sogginess.

Vacuum sealing is rarely ideal for very tender leaves, but light vacuum in a breathable pouch can slow oxidation. I label, date, and store at cool temperatures to extend shelf life without losing aroma.
