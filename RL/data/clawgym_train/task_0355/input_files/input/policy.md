# Coffee Shop Background Music Policy

Date effective: 2025-01-01

Purpose: Provide a pleasant, inclusive atmosphere for guests while ensuring legal and brand-safety compliance.

1. Business Hours for Music
- Play background music only during business hours: 06:00–22:00 local time.

2. Explicit Content
- Do not play tracks with explicit content between 06:00 and 17:00.
- Recommendation: Enable the streaming service’s explicit-content filter during daytime shifts (06:00–17:00).

3. Volume Guidelines
- Keep playback volume at or below 70 (as set on the player’s 0–100 scale) at all times.

4. Logging
- Maintain daily playback logs including timestamp, track, artist, genre, explicit flag, volume, and duration.

5. Review & Corrective Action
- Review logs weekly. If violations are found, curate clean versions, adjust schedules, and tune default volume.
