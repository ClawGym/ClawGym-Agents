# Week 7: Cell Biology & Scientific Practices

## Topic A
TopicID: BIO-1
Title: Cell Structure and Function
Objectives:
- Identify major organelles and their functions
- Differentiate plant vs. animal cell structures
MustIncludeTerms: cell membrane; nucleus; mitochondria
StudentFriendlyAnalogy: A cell is like a tiny city where each organelle has a job.
SuggestedHashtag: #Cells

## Topic B
TopicID: BIO-2
Title: Photosynthesis
Objectives:
- Describe inputs (CO2, H2O, light) and outputs (glucose, O2)
- Explain the role of chloroplasts
MustIncludeTerms: chlorophyll; glucose; oxygen
StudentFriendlyAnalogy: Plants are solar-powered chefs making sugar.
SuggestedHashtag: #Photosynthesis

## Topic C
TopicID: BIO-3
Title: Cellular Respiration
Objectives:
- Compare aerobic vs. anaerobic respiration
- Connect respiration to ATP energy
MustIncludeTerms: ATP; mitochondria; oxygen
StudentFriendlyAnalogy: Respiration is how cells “cash in” food for energy.
SuggestedHashtag: #Respiration

## Topic D
TopicID: BIO-4
Title: Mitosis vs. Meiosis
Objectives:
- Contrast purpose and outcomes (growth/repair vs. gametes)
- Recognize chromosome number changes
MustIncludeTerms: diploid; haploid; chromosomes
StudentFriendlyAnalogy: Mitosis makes body cell clones; meiosis shuffles decks for diversity.
SuggestedHashtag: #CellDivision

Title: Scientific Method
Objectives:
- Define hypothesis, variable, control
- Emphasize repeatability and evidence
MustIncludeTerms: hypothesis; variable; control
