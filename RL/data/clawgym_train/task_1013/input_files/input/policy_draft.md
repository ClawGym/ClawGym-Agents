# Team Code of Conduct (Draft)

## Purpose
We aim to foster respect, safety, and sportsmanship on and off the field.

## Behavior Expectations
Players should avoid unsportsmanlike conduct and show respect to opponents, officials, and teammates. Behavior that detracts from fair play may lead to appropriate action.

## Disciplinary Process
The coaching staff will decide on appropriate action when incidents occur. Options may include a warning or penalty. In severe cases, an ejection may be issued. Repeat offenses will be addressed case by case.

## Appeals
Players may appeal disciplinary decisions to the coaching staff within 48 hours of notification.

## Communication
Incidents will be tracked by the team. Players will be informed of decisions and any consequences.

This draft reflects our current understanding and is subject to improvement.
