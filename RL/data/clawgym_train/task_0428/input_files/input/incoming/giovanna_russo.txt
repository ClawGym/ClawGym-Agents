Oral History Transcript
Interviewee: Giovanna Russo
Interview Date: 1948-06-15
Birthplace: Naples, Italy
Occupation: Seamstress and community organizer
Key Contributions: Established a mutual aid society in Boston; Organized wartime relief drives; Mentored young Italian-American women
Summary: Giovanna discusses emigrating in 1921, working in Boston's garment district, and founding the Società di Mutuo Soccorso. She reflects on blending Italian traditions with American civic life.
