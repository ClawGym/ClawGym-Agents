# TradeRoots: A Platform Inspired by Historical Logistics

## Concept
Reimagine a platform that matches seasonal demand with stable routes inspired by the Silk Road, trans-Saharan caravans, and Mediterranean coastal shipping. The initial niche is spices and textiles across Central Asian corridors and the Indian Ocean, eventually expanding to adjacent commodities.

## Assumptions
- We can identify lower-risk corridors by seasonality and route characteristics.
- Matching known commodity flows with predictable tolls and risk should yield sustainable unit economics.
- Competitive pressure will be higher where there is already digital adoption.

## Open Questions
- Which commodity-route combinations are most economically attractive after adjusting for risk?
- Where do modern competitors overlap with historically strong commodity flows?
- How should we prioritize GTM regions and commodities based on evidence?
