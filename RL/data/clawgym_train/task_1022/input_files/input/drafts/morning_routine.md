# Morning Routine for Gentle Balance

I begin each day by listening to my intuition. A few breaths to center energy,
then slow movement to invite balance and harmony into the body. This practice is
about healing rhythms, not chasing metrics. With mindful breath and simple
herbal steams, the nervous system softens naturally.

I am not interested in forcing the body to comply with someone else’s framework.
Instead, I honor what arises and let the day unfold in alignment with inner
wisdom and mindfulness.
