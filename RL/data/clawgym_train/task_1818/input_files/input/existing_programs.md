# Riverview Foods, LLC — Existing Safety Programs (Status as of 2026-03-31)

Program | Status | Notes
--- | --- | ---
Emergency Action Plan (EAP) — 29 CFR 1910.38 | Implemented | Evac maps posted; annual drill last completed 2025-09.
Hazard Communication (HazCom/GHS) — 1910.1200 | Partially Implemented | SDS library ~85% current; chemical inventory not updated since 2025-08; some secondary containers unlabeled in sanitation.
Lockout/Tagout (Control of Hazardous Energy) — 1910.147 | Partially Implemented | Corporate policy exists; only 40% of equipment-specific procedures drafted; periodic inspections last done 2024; training provided to new hires but not all maintenance techs have annual refresher.
Powered Industrial Trucks (Forklifts/PIT) — 1910.178 | Implemented with Gaps | Initial training documented; some 3-year evaluations overdue; pre-use checklist logs inconsistent; pedestrians and PIT traffic separation needs improvement at docks.
Machine Guarding — 1910 Subpart O (1910.212) | Implemented with Gaps | Most guards present; interlock bypass observed during jams; need supervisor enforcement and jam-clearing SOPs.
Walking-Working Surfaces — 1910 Subpart D (1910.22) | Implemented with Gaps | Wet-floor controls adequate but sanitation foam creates slip hazard; more floor squeegees and anti-slip mats needed.
PPE Program — 1910 Subpart I (1910.132-.138) | Implemented | Gloves, cut-resistant sleeves in slicing; face shields for sanitation chemical handling; enforcement generally good.
Hearing Conservation — 1910.95 | Not Implemented | Noise monitoring not performed since 2023; suspected exposures near slicers and compressors.
Respiratory Protection — 1910.134 | Not Applicable (Limited Use) | Not required for normal operations; disposable masks used voluntarily for dusty spice handling; fit testing not required.
Confined Space Entry — 1910.146 | Partially Implemented | Inventory of permit-required spaces incomplete; locks on mixing tanks installed; no recent entries recorded.
Electrical Safety — 1910 Subpart S | Implemented with Gaps | Panel labeling complete; some missing dead-fronts reported for replacement.
Bloodborne Pathogens — 1910.1030 | Not Applicable | No occupational exposure anticipated.
Ergonomics (Voluntary) | Implemented with Gaps | Team lift policy exists; no formal ergonomic assessments performed in last 12 months.
Ammonia Refrigeration — Below PSM threshold | Implemented | Leak detection tested quarterly; contractors maintain system; emergency response integrated into EAP.

Key Gaps Identified:
- Equipment-specific LOTO procedures incomplete and annual audits overdue.
- PIT operator evaluations beyond 3-year cycle for 4 of 12 operators.
- HazCom: SDS library not fully current; secondary container labeling inconsistent.
- Slip hazards during sanitation due to foam residue and inadequate floor squeegee availability.
- Hearing Conservation: No recent noise survey; program absent.
- Pedestrian/forklift separation insufficient at shipping and receiving docks.
- Jam-clearing practices occasionally bypass interlocks; need lockout/clear/jog SOP and enforcement.
