# Product Strategy 2025

Our focus is to accelerate value delivery through usability, performance, and extensibility.

## Strategic Themes
1. Speed: Reduce median page load by 40% across core workflows.
2. Usability: Streamline onboarding and improve navigation clarity.
3. Extensibility: Expand integrations and improve developer tooling.

## Roadmap Highlights
- Q1: New dashboard architecture, accessibility improvements, and telemetry foundations.
- Q2: Advanced automation rules and improved search relevance.
- Q3: Analytics enhancements and collaboration features.
- Q4: Platform hardening and internationalization groundwork.

## Success Metrics
- Net retention > 120%
- Weekly active users +30%
- Support tickets per user -20%

## Risks and Mitigations
- Dependency on partner APIs: maintain fallbacks and clear SLAs.
- Scope creep: enforce definition of done and change control.

## Notes
- Continue investing in docs, tutorials, and community engagement.
