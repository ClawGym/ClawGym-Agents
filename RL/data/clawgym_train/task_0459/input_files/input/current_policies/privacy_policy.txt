Storefront Furniture Co. Privacy Policy (last updated: 2019-05-20)\n\nWhat we collect: For in-store purchases we collect customer name, phone number, optional email, street address for delivery, and itemized purchase details. Payment is processed by third-party card terminals in our stores; we do not store full credit card numbers or CVV.\n\nHow we use your information: We use contact details to schedule deliveries and provide warranty support. We do not sell customer information. Loyalty program enrollment is optional and may use contact details for occasional promotions.\n\nWhat we don't collect: We do not collect images, GPS/location data, detailed household information, or behavioral preferences. We do not maintain online accounts and we do not track browsing activity.\n\nData retention and security: Physical receipts and electronic POS records are retained for up to 24 months for accounting and warranty purposes. Access to POS terminals is restricted to authorized staff.\n\nThird parties: We share delivery addresses with our contracted delivery service solely for the purpose of completing deliveries.\n\nPolicy gaps may exist if we begin collecting new categories of data online (e.g., photos, metadata, online payments, or account information) that are not described here.\n
