# Field Notes — 2023-08-14

We sat by the bend of the river as dusk cooled the stones.

### Story: The River's First Laugh
Storyteller: Kanoa

"The river laughed like a child."
"We still bring gourds when the creek turns."

Some elders nodded as the shoreline darkened.

### Story: Origin of Fire
Storyteller: Anaï

"We learned to keep embers safe in clay."
"Children were taught to feed it like a guest."

A hush fell, and we could hear the insects begin their chorus.
