Product summary
- RelayHub connects Jira, Slack, ServiceNow, and Salesforce to remove manual handoffs across IT and Ops.
- New in v2.0: policy-aware workflows, native SOC 2 tooling, SSO with SAML/OIDC, and a visual builder.

Core value propositions
- Cut manual handoffs by 40% within 60 days using policy-aware routing.

- Secure by default: SSO (SAML/OIDC), role-based access, encryption at rest and in transit.
- Integrates natively: Jira, ServiceNow, Slack, Salesforce, GitHub, Okta.

FAQs
- Will it work with our SSO?
  - Yes. Native SAML and OIDC with Okta, Azure AD, and OneLogin.
- How long does implementation take?
  - Typical first workflow in 2 weeks; full rollout in 4–8 weeks.
- How is data secured?
  - SOC 2 controls, encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+), granular RBAC.
- Pricing?
  - Tiered by seats and workflows. Pilot pricing available for mid-market teams.
- Who owns maintenance?
  - Ops teams can manage workflows via the visual builder. Our team provides best-practice playbooks.
