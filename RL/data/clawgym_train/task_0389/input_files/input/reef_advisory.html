Coral Reef Advisory

Issuing authority: Okinawa Prefecture (Fisheries & Ocean Policy Division)

Level: Advisory Level 1 (Heat Stress)

Effective: 2023-07-05 to 2023-07-15

Affected area: Nearshore reefs around Motobu and Yagaji

  • Advice: Avoid anchoring on coral heads.
  • Reduce catch pressure on herbivorous fish.
  • Report any bleaching to the local cooperative.

Notes: Sea surface temperatures are expected to remain elevated for the next two weeks.
