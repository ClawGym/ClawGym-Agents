# Northstar Capital Brand Voice

Do not translate the brand or product names: "Northstar Capital" and "Northstar Flex" must remain in English in all locales.

Voice:
- Clear, confident, supportive. Avoid jargon and hype.
- Speak directly to the reader. Prefer short sentences.
- Spanish (es-ES): use "usted" forms. French (fr-FR): use "vous".

Formatting and placeholders:
- Do not alter placeholders in curly braces (e.g., {business_name}, {website_url}, {first_name}).
- Keep numbers and email addresses unchanged.

Consistency:
- Use the approved glossary for key financial terms.
- Maintain the same structure, headings, and list order as the source.
