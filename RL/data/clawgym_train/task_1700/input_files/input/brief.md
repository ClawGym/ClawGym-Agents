# Product Brief — Personal Finance App (codename: Finch)

## Context
We set out to build a personal finance app that makes money feel calm. Instead, we added too much. Today it looks like a mashup of budgeting, investing, and social features with unclear positioning. We need to cut to the essence.

## Audience
- Busy young professionals, 25–40, US/UK
- Salaried or freelance with variable income
- Tech comfortable but not power users
- Primary pain: daily money anxiety and decision fatigue

## Core Problem
People do not wake up wanting charts. They want an answer to one question: “Can I buy this today without hurting my month?” Current tools flood them with categories, graphs, and goals but fail to give a single clear yes or no.

## Insight
The human truth: money stress is a daily, low-grade ache. The cure is a single number that feels trustworthy and immediate. After bills and savings are handled, show what’s safe to spend today. Make that number feel inevitable.

## Vision
- One daily number that updates in real time
- Bills and savings handled automatically in the background
- A calm calendar that shows cash flow at a glance
- Nudges only when they matter
- Zero fluff, zero ads, ruthless focus

## Differentiation
- We are not a bank
- We are not a trading app
- We are not a social network
- Privacy-first: clear data boundaries, no resale
- Design that disappears, decisions made for you

## Current Feature Sprawl (symptoms)
- Trading and robo-advisor concepts crept in
- Crypto tracker and cashback marketplace in backlog
- Social feed and challenges explored
- Deep custom reporting planned
This noise hides the one thing we should do better than anyone.

## What V1 Must Do
- Link accounts securely and pull transactions reliably
- Forecast bills and set aside the right amounts
- Autopilot savings to goals and an emergency buffer
- Compute and display today’s Safe-to-Spend on home
- Show a cash flow calendar that builds trust
- Light, respectful nudges and a simple widget

## What V1 Will Not Do
- Investing or trading of any kind
- Social feeds, public leaderboards, or challenges
- Cash back shopping portals
- Deep custom reports and rule builders
- Multi-currency travel tooling
- Receipt OCR and expense filing

## Moments That Matter
- Morning check: “What can I spend today?”
- At the store: a quick glance settles the decision
- Before rent hits: calm confirmation that we’re covered
- Payday: automatic sweeps to goals without effort

## Success (first 90 days)
- Daily active use: 20 seconds or less, 5 days per week
- 25% increase in user savings rate
- 40% reduction in monthly overdraft incidents among connected users
- NPS > 60 driven by clarity and calm

## Voice & Feel
- Confident, declarative, human
- Short words, short sentences
- No jargon, no numbers that don’t matter
- Feels inevitable, not configurable

## Platform & Constraints
- iOS and Android, mobile-first
- Bank linking via reputable aggregator
- Encryption in transit and at rest
- No ads, no selling data

## North Star
Make money calm by giving one number you trust every day. Everything else either supports that or it goes.

---

### Working POV
If a feature does not make today’s number more trusted, clear, or useful in the moment, we remove it or ship it later. Real artists ship — but only the essential.
