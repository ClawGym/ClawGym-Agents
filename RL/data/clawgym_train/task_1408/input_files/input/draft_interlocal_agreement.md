# Interlocal Cooperation Agreement (Draft)

This Interlocal Cooperation Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by [LOCAL_GOV] and [PARTNER_GOV] for the purpose of [SERVICE_NAME], effective [EFFECTIVE_DATE].

## Parties and Purpose
The purpose of this interlocal arrangement is to coordinate resources between [LOCAL_GOV] and [PARTNER_GOV] to deliver [SERVICE_NAME] efficiently.

## Governance and Administration
The parties will establish a joint board to oversee administration of the shared services. Each party will designate a representative to the board and share administrative responsibilities.

## Cost Sharing and Funding
The parties agree to share the cost of operations according to a mutually agreed formula, with payment due quarterly.

## Indemnification and Liability
Each party agrees to indemnify the other to the extent permitted by law. Nothing herein waives any governmental immunity or limits liability protections.

## Dispute Resolution
If a dispute arises under this Agreement, the parties will first attempt mediation in good faith before pursuing other remedies.

## Statutory Authority
This Agreement is authorized by the Interlocal Cooperation Act.
