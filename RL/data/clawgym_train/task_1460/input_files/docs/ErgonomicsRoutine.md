# Ergonomics Routine (Draft)

## Current Routine
I draw on a 13-inch tablet at my desk, typically 7–9 hours/day. I take about 4–5 short breaks and try to stretch between chapters. I usually batch inking after pencils.

## Pain Points
Neck stiffness near the end of long sessions and occasional wrist soreness after heavy inking days.
