Guidance for formatting customer support messages:
- Do not change the casing of abbreviations or acronyms, especially when they appear mid-sentence.
- Do not alter URL strings; keep them intact without adding or removing characters or inserting spaces.
- Preserve brand and product names as listed below.

Abbreviations and acronyms to preserve:
- e.g., i.e., etc., a.m., p.m., vs., Mr., Mrs., Ms., Dr., St., Jr., Sr.
- FAQ, ETA, SSO, ID, API, SKU
- OK, Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4

Brand and product names to preserve (exact casing):
- AcmeCRM
- HelpBot Pro
- PayFast
- DocuSign
- Okta
- Zendesk

URL handling:
- Examples that may appear in transcripts:
  - https://support.acmecrm.com/reset?lang=en
  - https://help.acmecrm.com/t/abc-123
  - https://status.acmecrm.com
- Do not insert spaces into URLs or break them across lines.
- If punctuation is required at sentence end, prefer placing it after non-URL trailing words when possible.
