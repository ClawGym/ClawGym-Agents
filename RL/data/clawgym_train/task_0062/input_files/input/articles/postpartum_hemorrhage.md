---
title: "Recognizing and Responding to Postpartum Hemorrhage"
author: "Nana Doula"
tags: ["postpartum hemorrhage", "safety"]
---

Know the warning signs: soaking a pad within an hour, large clots, dizziness, or feeling faint. Seek urgent medical care if these occur.

Partners can help by timing pad changes and noting symptoms. Keep emergency numbers handy and trust your instincts.
