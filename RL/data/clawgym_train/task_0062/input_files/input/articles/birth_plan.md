---
title: "Writing a Birth Plan That Works for You"
author: "Nana Doula"
updated: "2023-01-15"
tags: ["birth plan", "communication"]
---

A birth plan is a conversation starter with your care team. Keep it concise, flexible, and focused on what matters most to you. Bring two printed copies, and review it at your prenatal visits.

Consider including preferences on labor positions, intermittent monitoring, delayed cord clamping, and immediate skin-to-skin after birth.
