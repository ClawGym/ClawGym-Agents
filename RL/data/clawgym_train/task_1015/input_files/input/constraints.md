# Constraints: FinCreators Guild

This community supports creators who educate the public on banking. We are not a law firm, regulator, or personalized advisory service. All participation must honor these constraints.

## 1) Legal & Compliance Boundaries

- Not individualized financial advice
  - All content is educational, general, and informational. No case-specific recommendations (“You should open X account”, “Switch to Y bank now”) based on an individual’s circumstances.
  - Prohibited: Directing a specific person to a specific bank/product for their unique situation; asking for or evaluating personal financial statements; advising on tax or legal matters.
- Disclaimers
  - Every template/script must include (a) “Not financial advice” and (b) “Check with your bank and local regulations; policies and rates change.” Include “as of [DATE]” when rates/fees are mentioned and cite sources.
- Claims & Sources
  - Rate/fee/FX claims must be timestamped and sourced (official bank pages, regulator sites, or reputable aggregators). Avoid unverifiable anecdotes.
  - No guarantees (e.g., “Guaranteed fee reversal” or “You will save $X”). Use ranges or “typical” with sources where possible.
- Vendor Neutrality & Conflicts
  - Disclose affiliate/sponsor relationships clearly. Avoid aggressive affiliate steering. Present multiple options where feasible.
  - No defamation or unsubstantiated negative claims about institutions.
- Jurisdiction & Product Limits
  - Use jurisdiction tags (e.g., [US], [UK/EU], [IN], [NG], [PH], [BR], [CA], [AU], [UAE]) on posts with localized information.
  - Avoid instructing members to circumvent KYC/AML or other legal processes.
- Handling Scripts & Guidance
  - Fee dispute scripts are allowed only as generic templates with placeholders (bank name, fee type, dates) and polite language. Include steps to verify bank policies first.
  - International wire guidance must compare typical options (SWIFT, SEPA, Wise/Revolut/Payoneer) with transparent cost components and a “verify with your provider” note.
- Platform ToS & Local Law
  - Members must comply with platform policies and their local laws. Moderation acts on violations regardless of intent.

## 2) Privacy & Data Protection

- No PII or account data
  - Prohibited: Account numbers, statements, screenshots with visible PII, card images, addresses, or transaction IDs that identify a person.
  - Redaction policy: If sharing a screenshot is necessary for education, fully redact names, IDs, and balances; otherwise, recreate with dummy data.
- Minimal data collection
  - Only collect what’s needed for community operations (e.g., email for premium billing). Do not store banking documents.
  - Analytics should be aggregate and anonymized. Opt-out honored.
- Recording & Consent
  - Events recorded only with explicit notice. Speakers can opt out. Remove PII during editing before publishing.
- Data retention
  - Delete reported PII leaks immediately and purge from backups at the next scheduled rotation. Document incidents and resolutions.

## 3) Global Audience & Localization

- Inclusive Coverage
  - Prioritize US, UK/EU, India, Nigeria/Kenya, Philippines, Brazil, Mexico, Canada, Australia, UAE, and Germany in early materials. Expand based on demand.
- Time Zones
  - Rotate live events across EMEA/APAC/AMER windows. Provide recordings and written summaries within 24 hours.
- Language
  - Primary language: English. Allow region tags and encourage plain-English explanations of local terms. Avoid US-centric assumptions when discussing “checking/savings”; include local equivalents where relevant.
- Currency & Units
  - Always include currency codes (USD, GBP, EUR, INR, NGN, PHP, BRL, MXN, CAD, AUD, AED). For comparisons, specify date, source, and rate basis.

## 4) Moderation & Safety (Banking-Specific)

- Allowed
  - General education, process checklists, rate/fee updates with sources, vendor-neutral comparisons, anonymized case studies, and generic scripts with disclaimers.
- Not Allowed
  - Personalized advice; doxxing institutions’ employees; illegal instructions; publishing leaked internal bank docs; unverified claims presented as fact.
- Escalation
  - Potential misinformation → move to moderation queue for source check within 24h.
  - Repeated self-promotional affiliate posts outside designated channels → delete + warning; repeat offenders muted/banned per policy.
  - Hate speech/harassment → immediate ban.
- Correction Protocol
  - Corrections should be public, constructive, and source-linked. Keep a changelog for templates.

## 5) Budget & Tooling Constraints (First 90 Days)

- Budget
  - Target ≤ $150/month. Prefer free tiers. Defer paid community platforms until PMF signals (≥45% D30 retention, ≥5% premium conversion among actives).
- Platform & Bots
  - Primary: Discord (free). Consider Circle/Mighty/Patreon + Discord roles for premium in Phase 2-3.
  - Bots: Minimal set (welcome, role assignment, simple reminders). No bots that store PII or financial data.
- Automation Ops
  - Health checks should include: process alive checks, responsiveness, version mismatch detection, cooldowns to prevent restart loops, a lock to prevent concurrent runs, and orphaned process cleanup. Send notifications without exposing PII.

## 6) Quality Bar & Review

- Source-first culture: Any claim about money must be sourced and dated.
- Peer review: New templates/scripts require one champion/mod approval.
- Update cadence: Rate/fee resources reviewed monthly; faster for volatile markets.
- Accessibility: Provide text summaries and captions for recordings.
