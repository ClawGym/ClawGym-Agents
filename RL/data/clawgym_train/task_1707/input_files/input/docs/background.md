Our business operates two retail locations in Northside and Downtown. We want to sponsor visible, community-supported green projects that improve walkability, shade, and stormwater management near those corridors.

Goals and constraints:
- Annual sponsorship budget cap: $60,000.
- Prefer projects near Northside and Downtown to align with our stores and staff volunteer base.
- Lean toward projects with shorter maintenance commitments (ideally 5 years or less).
- Visible impact matters for our customers and employees (shade trees and planters are great; stormwater features are welcome if signage is feasible).
- We'd like a simple local workflow we can run monthly without cloud services.
