Fresh Fish Packaging: Films, Barriers, and Drip Control

For seafood, oxygen barrier films slow oxidation and rancidity, while MAP (modified atmosphere packaging) can manage odor. I care about clean drip control and cold-chain integrity to protect texture.

Recyclable polyethylene options and biopolymer coatings are on my radar when they do not compromise freshness or safety. My goal is long shelf life with clean flavor.
