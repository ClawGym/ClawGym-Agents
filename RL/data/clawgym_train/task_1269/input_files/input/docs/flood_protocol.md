# Urban Flood Response Protocol (v2.0)

## Scope
Applies to riverine and flash flood conditions within city limits.

## Activation Triggers
- River gauge above flood stage for 2 consecutive readings.
- Flash flood warning in effect from the National Weather Service.

## Protective Actions
- Distribute sandbags at the Department of Public Works yard.
- Close low-lying roads and underpasses when inundation is observed.
- Issue evacuation guidance for Zone A if water level projections exceed threshold.

## Communications
- Push Wireless Emergency Alerts within 15 minutes of activation.
- Post a real-time road closure map on the city website.
