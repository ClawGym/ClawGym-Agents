Subject: Query: STARS ON MADISON (working title: Starlight on Madison)

Dear Ms. Bishop,

I’m reaching out with a project I’m excited about and would love to put on your radar because I think it aligns with your taste and my platform plans. My novel, Starlight on Madison, is a completed 82,000-word historical romance set in 1959–1961, exploring the electric world of mid-century advertising through a love story between a junior copywriter, Lila Hart, and an art director, Jack Fallon. Readers of workplace-forward romances who appreciate lush atmospherics and strong agency (pun not intended) will find themselves at home here.

Comparative titles include The Dud Avocado and a dash of ad-world glamour reminiscent of mid-century New York narratives. Early beta readers have responded with high engagement, and I’ve been building audience touchpoints across socials. The book examines ambition, creativity workflows, and brand voice alongside intimacy, with scenes that go from copy desk banter to neon-lit late nights.

I’d be thrilled to send the full manuscript at your request. Thank you for your time and consideration.

Warmly,

A. Rowan
