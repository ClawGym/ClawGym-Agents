Title: Sino–Japanese Normalization, 1968–1978: Documents and Perspectives
Date: 2026-05-22

Overview:
This reading session will focus on the lead-up to and aftermath of the 1972 normalization between the People’s Republic of China and Japan. I hope to circulate a small packet of primary documents (communiqués, official statements, legislative records, and archival items) as a basis for discussion on diplomatic priorities and public narratives in both countries.

Goal:
Gather a concise, bilingual-friendly set of authoritative materials to share with colleagues for preliminary discussion and to identify gaps for further archival work.
