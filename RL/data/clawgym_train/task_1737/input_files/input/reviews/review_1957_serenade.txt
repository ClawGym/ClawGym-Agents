Title: Silver Screen Serenade: A Grace Note to the Decade
Outlet: Cinema Gazette
Reviewer: Hector Vance
Date: 1957-11-08
Film: Silver Screen Serenade (1957)

An elegant production bathed in soft light.
Elliot Grant's performance is tired and stiff, saved only by the lush score.
Audrey Vale's voice is a marvel.
