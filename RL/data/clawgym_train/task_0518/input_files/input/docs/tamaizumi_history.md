Tamaizumi-ike is a small irrigation pond in Yamaguchi Prefecture, cherished by local families for seasonal walks and birdwatching.
The earthen embankment was reinforced in 2015, when the town added a simple gravel walking path around the shore.
Volunteers from the neighborhood association organize a spring cleanup around mid-April each year.
Informational and safety signage near the south entrance was noted as faded in 2022.
Residents commonly report sightings of carp, turtles, and herons.
