# Chapter 4 — Analysis

## 4.1 PEST Analysis
- Political/Regulatory: Content rules tighten periodically; approvals add lead time. Consumer protection raises bar for claims and return handling.
- Economic: Slower macro growth and higher platform costs increase price sensitivity; exchange rates affect import pricing.
- Social: Short-video consumption and KOL influence keep growing; younger consumers are promotion-seeking.
- Technological: Algorithmic distribution favors engagement; warehouse automation improves throughput but requires stable volume.

## 4.2 Company Strategy Analysis
NovaMart expanded assortment and invested in Tmall and Douyin visibility. It focused on KOL partnerships and festival campaigns to drive GMV. The strategy emphasizes traffic acquisition and reach.

## 4.3 4P Marketing Mix
- Product: Wide SKU range across beauty, wellness, and snacks; imported packaging.
- Price: Tiered discounting during major events; routine coupons.
- Place: Tmall Global (flagship), Douyin live-stream, and JD Worldwide.
- Promotion: Platform ads, KOL collaborations, bundles, and time-limited deals.

## 4.4 Porter’s Five Forces
- Threat of new entrants: Moderate; low listing barriers but brand-building takes time.
- Supplier power: Moderate; international brands require volume commitments.
- Buyer power: High; consumers compare prices and promotions.
- Substitutes: High; local brands and private labels.
- Rivalry: High; intensified by platform bidding and calendared festivals.

## 4.5 SWOT Summary
- Strengths: Global sourcing, brand trust, bonded warehouse network.
- Weaknesses: Slow content approval, higher cross-border shipping costs, less flexible pricing than locals.
- Opportunities: Live-stream commerce growth, niche imported SKUs.
- Threats: Local fast-copy rivals, platform policy shifts.

## 4.6 Campaign Observations (Descriptive)
Campaigns around 6.18 and Double 11 generated large GMV spikes but also higher return rates and stockouts. For example, next-day cancellation rates rose to ~14% coinciding with deep coupons. Rebates and KOL fees increased overall promotional intensity. Fulfillment cost per order in 2024 averaged RMB 12.3. Conversion rates in live-stream rooms varied widely; some top KOLs achieved 3.1% conversion versus 2.4% store average.

## 4.7 Preliminary Recommendations (High-Level)
- Strengthen localization, especially content adaptation.
- Enhance forecasting around campaigns to minimize stockouts.
- Deepen relationships with high-performing KOLs.
- Improve logistics efficiency and coordination with platforms.
