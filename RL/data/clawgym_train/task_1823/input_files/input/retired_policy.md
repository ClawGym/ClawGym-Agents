# Retired Senator Blake’s Clean Energy Legacy Plan (Draft)

- Target: Move to 80% renewable electricity by 2030.
- Permitting: Stop new oil and gas permits after 2028.
- Carbon fee: Start at $25/ton in 2026, rising to $50/ton by 2030.
- Household impact: Due to efficiency rebates and innovation, the average household will save about $300/year by 2030.
- Reliability: Maintain reliability with no interruptions.
