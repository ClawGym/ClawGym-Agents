# April Onboarding Announcement

Welcome to the April onboarding cycle! We are excited to welcome new teammates across engineering, product, and operations. This pack contains the essentials for getting started quickly and consistently.

## Key Dates
- Orientation kickoff: April 8, 2026
- Security training deadline: April 12, 2026
- First sprint pairing: April 15, 2026

## What’s Included
- Account provisioning checklist
- Access request guidance
- Team communication norms
- Quick links to docs and dashboards

## Action Items for New Joiners
1. Complete your profile and confirm your preferred display name.
2. Set up multi-factor authentication on all required services.
3. Review the engineering handbook and coding standards.
4. Join your team’s Slack channels and subscribe to the announcements channel.

If you have any questions, please reach out in the #it-support channel or contact your onboarding buddy directly.

Thank you, and welcome aboard!
