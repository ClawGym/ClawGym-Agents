Neuromuscular Disease Pharmacotherapy (Clinic A)

Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis (ALS)

Recommended disease-modifying therapies:

  • Riluzole
  • Edaravone

Supportive care may include non-invasive ventilation, gastrostomy, and antispasmodics.

Myasthenia Gravis (MG)

Recommended therapies for generalized MG:

  • Pyridostigmine
  • Prednisone
  • Azathioprine

Additional agents may be used based on severity and comorbidities.
