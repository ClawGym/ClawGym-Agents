# Tone & CTA Guidance (Trust + Clarity)

- Purpose: Help customers understand key topics with transparency, not sales copy.
- Tone: Clear, calm, factual, and reassuring. Avoid hype and urgency.
- Clarity: Prefer plain language. Define terms when useful.
- Trust cues: Reference official pages, summarize what customers can expect, and highlight ways Santander International explains protections and processes.
- CTAs: Invite readers to learn more or see details on official pages. Avoid pressure.

Examples of acceptable CTA copy:
- "Learn more about how we help protect you"
- "See our fees and charges explained"
- "Find support options that fit your needs"
