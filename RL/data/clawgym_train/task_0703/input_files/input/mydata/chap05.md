# Chapter 5 — Conclusions

## 5.1 Summary of Findings
NovaMart China has grown GMV through multi-platform expansion and increased promotional efforts. The market remains attractive but competitive, with consumers sensitive to deals and influenced by KOLs. Regulatory reviews require careful content planning. Operational complexity rises during campaign peaks.

## 5.2 Recommendations
- Strengthen localization of content and creative to improve CTR.
- Optimize inventory planning ahead of major festivals to reduce stockouts.
- Continue to partner with top KOLs and negotiate better revenue-sharing terms.
- Invest in logistics efficiency and explore bonded warehouse upgrades.
- Improve data dashboards for near-real-time decision making.

## 5.3 Limitations
The analysis is high-level and descriptive, utilizing standard frameworks. It does not provide causal testing or rigorous statistical inference. Future research can expand to primary surveys and econometric modeling.

## 5.4 Outlook
The company should maintain growth momentum while refining operations and marketing to better align with platform dynamics. Continued focus on consumer insights and agile execution will help sustain performance.
