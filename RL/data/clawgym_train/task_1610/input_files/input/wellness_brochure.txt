AcmeCo WellLife Program — Employee Brochure (v1.2)

Overview
WellLife is an optional program designed to help employees build healthy habits. You can participate using a compatible wearable or the WellLife mobile app.

Incentives
• Annual premium reduction: $300 for completing quarterly check‑ins.
• Monthly raffles for participants who meet personal goals.

What’s Included
• Personalized coaching via in‑app chat and quarterly webinars.
• Habit tracking: steps, resting heart rate, and sleep duration.
• Self‑reported check‑ins on nutrition, stress, and activity.

Data at a Glance
• Data types we track: steps, heart rate, sleep duration, and questionnaire responses.
• No GPS or precise geolocation data is collected.
• We use aggregated, anonymized insights to improve WellLife’s design.

Time Expectations
• Estimated weekly time commitment: 45–60 minutes.

Participation & Opt‑Out
• Participation is voluntary and can be stopped at any time.
• Your health coverage and employment status are unaffected by non‑participation.

Support
• In‑app chat support: weekdays 9am–6pm.
• Email support available for account issues.

Security Snapshot
• Data are encrypted in transit and at rest.

Contact
For questions about the program, contact HR or the WellLife support team.
