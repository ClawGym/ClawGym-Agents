Company: OnboardIQ
Stage: Seed-backed B2B SaaS (founded 2025)
Team: 14 (3 PM/Design, 6 Eng, 2 Data, 3 GTM)
ICP: Product-led B2B SaaS companies with 20–500 employees using self-serve trials or freemium.
Primary Users: Product managers, Growth/RevOps analysts, Customer Success managers.

Product
- Onboarding analytics focused on activation and time-to-value (TTV).
- Connects to event streams (Segment, RudderStack, SDKs), CRM (Salesforce/HubSpot), and support tools.
- Provides onboarding funnels, cohort analysis, and “stuck account” signals.

Value Proposition
- Specialized for onboarding: faster instrumentation, clear activation definitions, and proactive alerts.
- Templates by vertical (DevTools, Fintech, HR SaaS) to reduce setup time and avoid “blank dashboard” syndrome.
- CS/GTM workflows (alerts + playbooks) to intervene before users churn during onboarding.

Business Goals (H2 2026)
- Increase customer activation (14-day) from 33% to 45%.
- Reduce median TTV from 9.5 days to ≤6.5 days.
- Raise D30 team retention from 41% to ≥48%.
- Expand top-of-funnel by integrating with CRM for success workflows; improve NRR through earlier saves.

Revenue Model
- Tiered SaaS by Monthly Tracked Accounts (MTA) + add-ons for CRM integrations and advanced alerting.
- Starter ($299/mo), Growth ($799/mo), Scale ($1.9k+/mo), annual discounts 15–20%.

Competitive Landscape (high-level)
- Direct: June.so, InnerTrends, Appcues Analytics modules (for onboarding), Gainsight PX.
- Indirect: Mixpanel/Amplitude (general analytics), BI tools (Looker/Mode), building internal dashboards.
- Alternative: Do nothing—teams rely on ad hoc spreadsheets, delayed insights, and reactive CS outreach.

Key Company Priorities
- Time to first insight < 24h via templated activation definitions and no-code event mapping.
- Proactive “stuck account” detection with actionable playbooks (CSM and PLG-friendly).
- Strong Salesforce/HubSpot integration to push health signals into GTM workflows.

Constraints & Risks
- Small eng team; need to prioritize high-impact, low-effort features.
- Data instrumentation complexity is the primary friction during onboarding.
- Avoid becoming a generic analytics platform—stay focused on activation/TTV.

Q3 Themes
- Outcome focus: Activation lift, faster TTV, scalable CS interventions.
- Differentiation: Templates, assistive setup, and CRM-connected alerts.
