# Subscriptions

These are the shows I actively follow.

- Lex Fridman Podcast — long-form interviews on AI, science, and philosophy
- Huberman Lab — neuroscience-based tools for everyday life
- The Tim Ferriss Show — world-class performers unpacked
- Acquired — deep dives on great companies and the playbooks behind them
- Planet Money — stories that make economic ideas clear
- Darknet Diaries — true stories from the dark side of the Internet
- All-In — tech, markets, and politics roundtable
- 99% Invisible — design, architecture, and the hidden world of things

Notes:
- Prefer science/tech, business strategy, and practical takeaways.
- Flag notable guests from my watchlist for prioritization.
- Weekend listening window is usually ~3 hours.
