Title: Heritage Mask (Ink)
Year: 2020
Medium: Ink on textured paper
Notes: Study of ceremonial mask patterns and carved contours.
Motifs: Spirals, chevrons, and woven borders.
Technique: Dry brush to lift texture; wet passes for deep blacks.
Context: Referenced oral histories and community sketches.
Detail: Recorded variations in forehead crest and cheek markings.
Outline: Thick-to-thin strokes following grain direction.
