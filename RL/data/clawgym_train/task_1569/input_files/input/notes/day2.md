# Field Notes — 2023-08-15

Clouds gathered beyond the ridge; we met under the longhouse eaves.

### Origin Story: The Moon Sews the Night
Storyteller: Rūko of Eel Clan

"The moon's thread is thin but patient."
"We stitch darkness so stars may be seen."

### Story: Stone That Walks
Storyteller: Tala (River Children)

"The stone follows those who do not greet it."
"We place tobacco where it rests."
