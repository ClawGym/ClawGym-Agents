Brand voice and required elements:
- Tone: warm, courteous, plain language; no emojis or slang.
- Aim for 120–160 words.
- Subject line format: [Event Name] in Springfield — Invitation for {FirstName}
- The email body must include:
  1) Greeting with the client's first name.
  2) Event name, date (YYYY-MM-DD), time (HH:MM), and location exactly as in events.csv.
  3) One-sentence value proposition tailored to status:
     - Renters: getting ready to buy and understanding the path to homeownership.
     - Homeowners: simplifying, right-sizing, and practical steps to downsizing.
  4) Clear call to action: "Reply to this email to save your seat."
  5) Signature block:
     Best regards,
     Alex Martinez, Realtor
     Main Street Realty
     (555) 123-4567
  6) Disclaimers (include both lines verbatim):
     This invitation is informational; there is no obligation to attend.
     If you received this by mistake, please disregard.
