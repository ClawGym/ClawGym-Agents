Company: Pulseline
Product: Team Health Analytics for Engineering Managers

What we do
- Turn Jira/GitHub activity and optional lightweight Slack check-ins into weekly “manager digests” with early warning signals (burnout risk, review bottlenecks, delivery risk).
- Provide team-level trendlines (lead time, PR throughput, WIP) to make standups and sprint reviews more objective.

Target segment (ICP)
- Seed to Series B companies with 25–200 engineers.
- Buyers: Eng Managers and VPEs who own delivery predictability and team well-being.
- Tools: Jira or Linear + GitHub/GitLab; Slack.

Why now
- Post-remote engineering teams struggle with hidden risk and burnout.
- Managers lack time to synthesize signals across tools; they default to ad-hoc spreadsheets.

Current traction (last ~6 weeks)
- 12 trials; activation around 55–65% with manual onboarding.
- 3–4 active PoCs; 2 paying accounts on per-manager pricing.
- WAU hovering in the ~40–60 range; early signs that manager digests resonate.
- Friction points: Slack check-in noise; privacy concerns from compliance-conscious orgs; unclear next steps after alerts.

Distribution and sales motion
- Founder-led sales and onboarding, focusing on warm intros and lightweight referrals.
- No paid acquisition yet; small content efforts planned.
- Trials run 2–4 weeks with weekly touchpoints.

Pricing (subject to experiment)
- $60/manager/month; typical account has ~10 managers.
- Considering per-team pricing for larger orgs to reduce friction.

Risks and unknowns
- Retention: Do managers come back weekly and act on signals?
- Value confirmation: Would users be upset if Pulseline disappeared?
- Growth: Is there organic word-of-mouth beyond a handful of referrals?
- Compliance: Some leads ask for SOC 2; we plan to document data handling first and defer full certification.

Focus for next 1–2 quarters
- Improve retention by making digests clearly actionable and reducing false positives.
- Clarify privacy posture with a public data handling/DPIA summary.
- Test referral nudges once retention KPIs clear a threshold.

Founders’ time reality
- ~60 hours/week combined; must bias to manual-first tactics (concierge onboarding, direct interviews) and defer scale work until retention strengthens.
