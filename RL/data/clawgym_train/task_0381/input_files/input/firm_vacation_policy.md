Firm Time Away Policy (Associates)

- Annual PTO allotment: 200 hours per calendar year (front-loaded at the start of the year).
- Short breaks: Requests for up to 3 consecutive business days off do not require partner pre-approval when scheduled at least 3 business days in advance and reasonable coverage is arranged.
- Recharge recommendation: Each associate is strongly encouraged to take at least one uninterrupted 5-day (Mon–Fri) period off per year.
- Mental health: PTO may be used for mental health without explanation.
- Blackout dates: Refer to team calendars; e.g., litigation trial weeks may be blacked out.
- Process: Submit requests in the HRIS and notify your staffing coordinator for coverage.
- Carryover: Up to 40 hours may carry over to the next year.
