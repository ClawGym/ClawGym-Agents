Title: Observations on the Health of Factory Hands
Author: Thomas, Apprentice Mechanic
Last updated: 1850-01-01

Introduction
------------
In the bustle of mills and foundries, we have begun to tally ailments and mishaps to better understand the toll of work and the promise of improved machinery and ventilation. This pamphlet shares early observations intended for foremen, stewards, and the curious apprentice.
