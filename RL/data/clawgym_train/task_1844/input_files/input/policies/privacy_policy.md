Riverside Film Society – Privacy Policy
Last updated: 2023-10-01

Who we are
Riverside Film Society is a volunteer-run community group screening classic films at Riverside Community Hall.

Data we collect
When you join our mailing list or become a member, we collect your name and email address.

How we use your data
We use your contact details to send screening updates, manage membership, and coordinate volunteer shifts.

Lawful basis for processing
We rely on your consent to process your personal data. You can withdraw consent at any time.

Data retention
We retain your contact details for 12 months after your membership lapses or you unsubscribe, whichever comes first.

Your rights
You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data.

Contact us
For privacy questions or requests, email privacy@riversidefilmsoc.org.
