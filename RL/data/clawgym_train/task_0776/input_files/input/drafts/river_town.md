---
title: River Town
tempo: 84
key: C
instruments: [piano, fiddle, bass]
feel: lilting waltz
structure: verse-verse-bridge
---

Down in the river town, the mill whistle keeps the hours.
The story winds through porches, across the kitchen floor.
No chorus, just the verses hum like oars against the shore,
Names traded in the dusk and nothing more.
